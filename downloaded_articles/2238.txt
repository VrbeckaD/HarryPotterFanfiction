Loki and Thor rule as the two kings of the Nine Realms. </p><p>During a visit to Midgard Loki discovers something… interesting. Chaos is wrought, destiny is rewritten, and Asgard gains an heir as Loki takes the child as his own. There are still many enemies across the universe to Midgard and the many realms.
