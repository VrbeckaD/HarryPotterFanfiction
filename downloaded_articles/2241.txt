      <p>A year has passed since Voldemort's reign of terror on Wizarding Europe ended and no is happier than Draco Malfoy. Now, two days after his 18th birthday and a few weeks before he is set to head to Hogwarts for his 8th year, he is brought to the mysterious Mezereti estate where he meets and falls head over heels with his bride to be Ottaline Mezerti. He discovers that the tales that his parents had told him are true as Ottaline and her family reveal that they are fairy royalty. As time goes on and their love begins to grow as true mates, their love is tested and strained as new a threat appears. Will they succeed to create their own fairytale ending, or will they crash and burn without being by each other's side forever?</p>
