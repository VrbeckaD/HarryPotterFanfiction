The rejection had been tormenting, always getting what he wanted was normal and when Harry had denied him it hurt as if he wasn't good enough for “Saint Potter”. Pushing the old memories aside, he wiped a small tear away from his cheek and decided that going back to Hogwarts to finish his education would be his best chance at getting a job, given his family name’s bad reputation.
