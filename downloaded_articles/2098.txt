      <p>Between 2013 and 2014 I had a request series called All About Harry. This work compiles most of my unfinished stories. So let me be clear: THEY ARE ALL INCOMPLETE. The Marvel Cinematic Universe is disproportionally represented. I've cleaned them up a bit before posting.</p><p>Get ready for Harry to pop up in different universes, lots of time-travel, new friends and lovers made and long lost family found. And fusions in which Harry was always a part of that world.</p><p>If any one story stands out, and you'd like to read more, feel free to try to persuade me to complete it.</p>
