    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Draco Malfoy returns to England after having left the country nearly six years before when a series of cases that don't appear like they have any connection to each other starts popping up in England. These cases lead to him and the team he brings with him to pair up with Harry and some other Aurors. But when the cases go from simple theft to disappearances and even murder, fear soon sweeps over the British Isles causing concern to rise that Voldemort's impact on the wizarding world hasn't disappeared completely. But for Draco, he soon learns that some secrets that he'd kill to remain in the past will always be dragged to the light. Secrets that find he and Harry entangled in a complicated web of lies, mysteries, and shadowy organizations.</p>
