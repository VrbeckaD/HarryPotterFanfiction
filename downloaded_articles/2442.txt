    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>He struggles to hold on. His broken, bleeding soul slipping from his desperate grasp as his lost family struggled to heal the wounds his tormentors left upon scarred flesh. He cannot breathe, blood seemingly pouring from stinging wounds. This is the end, he knows. He will not live another day after this moment. And so, he bows his head and weeps for the loss of life. But he knows, in his heart, that death will end his suffering.</p><p>If he knew his nameless savior was finally on their way, he would have fought back.  </p><p>But he is so, so tired of fighting. And he knows, in his soul, that he must continue on. The second ruler has risen.</p><p>“His soul feeling as though a hole has been ripped into it with a dagger made of pure silver, bleeding from pain so raw that a tear slips down his skin, leaving behind a trail of sorrow so strong it would sadden a million men.”</p><p> </p><p>Edit: Due to a busy schedule  (College Classes and Show animals) I am sadly abandoning this work. If you would like, it can be adopted.<br/>EDIT 2.0**** I am slowly beginning to re-edit Ch1 and write the rest.</p>
    </blockquote>
