      <p>Death thought that he had managed to avert the crisis rather well, all things considered. Harry Potter was dead, sure, but the boy's lack of a beating heart was hardly that much of a issue in the greater scheme of things. Really, it was Death's personal opinion that his master had been improved upon after his death, now that he was without pesky things like organs or blood. </p><p>Though, it certainly brought in a great deal of questions regarding the boy's mortality when he got up every morning to eat breakfast.</p>
