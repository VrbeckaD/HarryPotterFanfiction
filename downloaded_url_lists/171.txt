And alone. <br/>It wouldn't stop him from carving out his identity , even if for the moment he was mildly..  magically crippled( thank you, dear author). Not helpless though. Not this proud necromancer. <br/>That was all he had cared about, in the beginning. Later though-<br/>Harry wasn’t even thinking of playing, but the others were already readying their pawns and castles.</p>
