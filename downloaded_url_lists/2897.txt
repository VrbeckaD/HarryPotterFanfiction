He's content, or so he tells himself, with a quiet life away from the bad memories, studying fascinating and beautiful creatures from all over the world.<br/>Thousands of feet above him, however, the TARDIS is burning- and a mysterious, bizarre, unexplainable woman is about to crash out of the sky and into Newt's life. Unfortunately, they're about to cross paths with something much less friendly. As the Doctor struggles to navigate her regeneration (her shoes are too small and she's craving hotdogs), and Newt grapples with his first human connection in years, can they find themselves- and the deadly threat, lurking in the museum- before it's too late?</p>
