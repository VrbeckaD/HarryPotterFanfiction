    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>***After DH's book***</p>
<p>"It had all started on a lazy Saturday in late July, the day Hayley Potter, his wife of ten years now, celebrated her thirtieth birthday to be exact.<br/>Severus was used to the oddities of his life partner, but when he had seen her return, her twin and Malfoy in tow, with an unconvinced expression and a colourful box in her hands, more than certainly Muggle-made, he had wondered if domesticity had made him a little dumber."<br/>-<br/>"In his now long and happy married life, there was a residual room, inside his heart, that he had never opened. A dark, dusty room, filled with memories as painful as sharp blades in living flesh.<br/>Severus never spoke of his childhood, never mentioned his father and mother. He shunned any reference to the Muggle world, where he had lived the hardest years of his life.<br/>And Hayley, by the least intrusive method she could have imagined, had tried to exorcise that pain, to give that room a good scrubbing."</p>
<p> </p>
<p>It's the translation of an Italian fanfiction, but I changed some parts.  Hayley Potter is my OC from "Rude", but you don't really need to read it first.</p>
    </blockquote>
