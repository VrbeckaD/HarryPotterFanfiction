    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>He was young- around Remus's age, surely, with high cheekbones and dark, wavy hair. His eyes were closed in concentration as he played the old piano, a slight smile playing at his lips as his fingers glided across the ivory keys. Though Remus had never seen him play here, he held himself like he owned the room- like he was the star of the show, if you will.</p><p>Remus swallowed, eyes glued to the young man. Was this him? The words on his arm seemed to be tingling. He didn't even realize he was staring until the pianist had opened his eyes and was meeting his gaze. He flashed a smile.</p><p>Feeling his cheeks flush, Remus looked away as his drink was placed down in front of him, grateful for new focus. The pianist must have known that he had been staring. He drained half of his drink before gathering his courage and glancing back at the young man. He had turned his attention back to his instrument, but was smiling smugly.</p><p>Well.</p><p>--<br/>Remus knows exactly three things about his soulmate. One is that they'll meet at a bar. Another is that he plays piano. The last is that he's a star- presumably the famous sort, not a literal one. Remus finds himself frequenting the local piano bar quite a lot.</p>
    </blockquote>
