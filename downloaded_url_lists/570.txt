    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>There have always been whispers of old magic in the woods.  Some say that you could make a promise to an ancient creature for whatever your heart desires. Hermione should have expected that the deal would have a price, especially when the creature that appeared to her in the forest had been alight with magic.  </p><p>Hermione could never have known that the oath she broke as a child would threaten to destroy everything that she worked for and everyone that she loves.  Now, a decade later, she comes to realize that nothing is as it seems and she has been set upon a path of no return.  Magic.  Betrayal.  Intrigue. Creatures of Old Magic that defy all reason.  All of those impossible things pale in comparison to what she has become. Neither fae nor completely human- struggling to stay alive as a war rages around her. With the fate of realms hanging in the balance, she will have to make a choice that will change everything.  She can only hope to make the right one, even if it breaks her heart.</p>
