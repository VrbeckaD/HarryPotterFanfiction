    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>The portrait of Cygnus Black I that hung in the Ministry reported that her firstborn had been sent to Azkaban without any trial whatsoever.</p>
<p>It made Walburga furious. Not on <em>His</em> behalf, of course, but because of the utter disrespect shown to a scion of the Noble and Most Ancient House of Black. How dare they? If she let them get away with it, then next time those filthy blood traitors might think it was perfectly fine to treat that way a <em>worthy </em>member of the House of Black. After all, it had set a precedent. A bad precedent. It mustn’t be allowed. </p>
<p>Or,</p>
<p>Walburga Black saves Sirius from Azkaban, and  it changes everything.</p>
    </blockquote>
