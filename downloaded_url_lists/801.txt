    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>This AO3 ''book'' will serve as reference for anyone willing to indulge in the post-war AU that I am currently creating.<br/>I have altered who dies as I am not happy about my favourites dying and as I have seen, many others aren't happy with it either.</p><p>My main page is on Instagram where I will showcase my art and do most of my interactions if this does gain traction!<br/>@PawsOneLilArt<br/>https://instagram.com/pawsonelilart?igshid=j5g3dyj5snnk</p><p>The AU will include:<br/>- major character death erasure<br/>- new main character trio; A French girl from a family of dragon riders, a werewolf who has connections with the Lupin family and Delphini re-written<br/>- more in depth about; The Lupin Family, The noble house of black, snippets of the marauders era, magical creatures (both new and canon) and other houses that weren't shown too much in the original series.<br/>- new characters in general, new teachers, curses, classes and modifications to the school to make it more inclusive!<br/>- AND I HAVE SO MUCH MORE PLANNED!</p><p>If this smidge of a tease seems interesting, then I welcome you to this project of mine!</p>
