    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>If the Ministry Of Magic, in the UK at least, outsourced their muggle related problems to a private firm what would it be like to work there?  Any time the Ministry needs an excuse for something else that has happened threatening the lives of countless Magical users then the Firm steps in.  Yes it has a creepy name, just the Firm because it is über powerful and in control of a lot.  The Firm is staffed by characters from other fandoms, so step in have a read and see who you can recognise.  I promise it’s going to be fun, I can promise you that!</p>
    </blockquote>
