    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>I previously had two works up here before, but due to an unhealthy past relationship my account and works are now gone. I am back, and while I don't have the original works, I am trying my hand at fanfiction again.</p>
<p>So, I never liked several parts of the Harry Potter universe, so I've re-imagined it the way I wold like it to have been. </p>
<p>This is really a prequel for a future work, I tell the story of the first war and that of Severus Snape finding love in the midst of the war. Severus is no longer the heart broken, miserable man, he deserved better than Lily Potter.</p>
<p>I write Slytherins being Slytherins, but that isnt a bad thing.</p>
    </blockquote>
