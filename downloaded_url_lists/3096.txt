    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>As he left the great hall, Tom rested against the wall in the hallway. His heart was thumping wildly and his breathing just wouldn't stop. Oh lord. He placed his hand over his heart to try to calm it down. Tom was supposed to be an emotionless knight. Love wasn't in his system. He took an oath to protect the king and not to fall in love with anyone other than his duties.</p><p>But, by gods... No one had ever made a heartwarming impression like Harry had done. Tom raised his hand—the one Harry kissed—and closed his eyes as he clenched his hand.</p><p>Under any circumstances, Tom could not fall in love.</p><p>Not when he had other plans to take care of.</p><p>(on hiatus)</p>
    </blockquote>
