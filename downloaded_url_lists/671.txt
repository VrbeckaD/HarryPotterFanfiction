    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Amadeus wouldn't have chosen England or Hogwarts if it was up to him. Not at all. He would have chosen Dumstrang - where only the strong survive - or maybe Mahoutokoro where the smart and agile reigned. </p><p>If it was really up to him, he would've gone for the Japanese school in order to get closer to his mother's roots. Plus, he still remembered how amazing and beautiful the school was from when he was a child. But his brother always told him that Dumstrang wasn't all that terrible. However, his brother was a force of nature - physically and mentally - so he wasn't so sure about his advice. </p><p>But he knew one thing. He wouldn't have chosen Hogwarts. It was boring. The students were boring, the teachers were boring, the place itself was boring. </p><p>At last, he found that only one person was worse of his attention. Tom Riddle. The poor but brilliant orphan. That was entertainment. </p><p>Maybe, just maybe, Amadeus time at Hogwarts won't be so dull after all.</p>
    </blockquote>
