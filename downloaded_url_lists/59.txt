    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Harry grew up with the need to prove his so-called family wrong about him. He was smart, cunning, sly, and ambitious. What happens when the Boy Who Lived comes to Hogwarts with a mentality to not trust without proof? What happens when this distrust drives him to be cautious of everything said about the magical world? What happens when on the train Draco proves he can be trusted? What happens when Harry knew about the magical world thanks to a snake named Nathair? How will everything change?</p>
    </blockquote>
