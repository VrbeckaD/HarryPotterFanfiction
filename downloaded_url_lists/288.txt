    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Ron's an auror, he's assigned a junior partner for six months - Draco Malfoy. He brings Malfoy to drinks one week and Harry can't stop blushing... who knows why ?</p>
<p>"I’m fairly certain he’s aware that you’re attracted to him, I think everyone in this bar knows that.” <br/>The bartender passing by mumbles a quiet agreement and Harry turns bright red.<br/>“I have to go. I have to change my name and leave the country, right? This can’t happen!” <br/>“Harry, calm down. Nothing is happening.”<br/>“But it will, I can only sit next to him and not jump his bones for so long, Ron.” Harry says miserably. “As soon as he shows up, I start sweating.” His voice drops to a whisper. “I’m half hard just hearing his voice.” <br/>“Okay, that’s plenty of information, thanks.” Ron grimaces.</p>
    </blockquote>
