    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Harry is finally returning to Hogwarts for his fourth year, however he isn't greeting the year with the joy typical of him. Instead the boy is a shell of the one they previously knew, he jerks from even the most friendly touches, won't look anyone in the eyes and refuses to so much as make a sound. With the stress of the Tri-Wizard Tournament and everyone trying to get him to go back to how he was the year is going worse and worse for Harry. After getting changed to Slytherin, his new housemates are determined to help the boy no matter the previous nature of their relationship. But what they don't realize is the biggest threat to Harry right now is his own mind.</p>
    </blockquote>
