    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Harry returns from his visit to the Kings Cross afterlife, determined to make changes after the war. A world that allowed Voldemort to come to power, to begin with.</p><p>Harry decides he will wield his fame with the help of several advisers, many of whom despise one another and don't share the same beliefs.  He gets into politics and starts a charity that covers plenty of causes he believes in.</p><p>Harry is driven, outspoken, and confident about doing what's right, but he has horrific nightmares, personal self-confidence issues, and an inability to find peace on his own. The traumas of the first seventeen years of his life finally have a chance to catch up to him after Voldemort's final defeat. </p><p>Harry finds his match in Severus, the one who can relate to things Harry's been through better than any other.
