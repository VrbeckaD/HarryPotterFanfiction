    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>
  <em>When you look at it closely, the cracks and plot holes that appear in the Harry Potter series make me think that the whole thing is really just a cover story concocted by the powers that be because the TRUE story is too subversive to tell. </em>
</p>

That conflict seems to have fed two wizard wars.</em></p>
<p>  <em>I can't believe the Founders meant that to happen, so what was the balancing element? The 5th House. Read on to see why...</em></p>
