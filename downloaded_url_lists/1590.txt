    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Harry has had enough of the repeated abuse, the excuses, the yearly challenges. So when he’s offered the chance to surrender and cease the constant pain and torment... he graspes it with both hands and won’t let go. </p><p>Voldemort had given the offer flippantly, purely meant as a distraction so he would have time to think of something better. He expected a rant about how the Golden Boy would never join someone like him, he expected a fight, he expected - something.  But what he got was the complete opposite. </p><p>A year after this exchange Voldemort comes for him, finally ready to make good on his promise, Harry practically runs to him. But both are going to find their expectations of each other crushed as truths come to light and minipulations are revealed.</p>
    </blockquote>
