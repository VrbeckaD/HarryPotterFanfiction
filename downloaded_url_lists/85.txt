    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>An alternate universe reimagining of Lupin + Tonks, in which they could maybe — <em>just maybe</em> — have a chance at a life together.</p><p>This idea came from a canon compliant fic I wrote about our favorite werewolf and his doomed love. Since their fate in the books is pretty devastating, I got a few requests for a happier remix. Some scenes have parallels to the other story, but everything happens slightly differently this time around, and these small differences change everything that comes next.</p>
