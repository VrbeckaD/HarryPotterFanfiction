      <p>____________________________<br/>Can you remember<br/>Who you were,<br/>Before the world<br/>Told you who you should be?<br/>____________________________</p><p>The life of the Malfoy twins was dark and cold. No affection shown and not even the lights could brighten up the Manor. Cassiopeia was raised to never show herself her true self out in the world while Draco was raised to be the Malfoy heir. So Cassiopeia made sure that though her brother was fed lies about muggles and half bloods, she would tell him the truth, that blood was blood. It changed nothing.</p><p>They both had one wish they shared, which was to be known as siblings. Going to Hogwarts together was their one way ticket to paradise. Except their father tells them he registered them with different last names, one Black and one Malfoy. Their dream crashes and burns fast. </p><p>But Cassiopeia was not going to let her brother be fed anymore lies and if that means she has to break some of the Malfoy rules then so be it. In her book family came first and nothing was getting in her way.</p><p>FIRST BOOK</p>
