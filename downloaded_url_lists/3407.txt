    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>The Firebug books are an original set of post-canonical stories set an indeterminate but significant time after the Harry Potter books. Chiân Maeroris is an original character, who receives her Hogwarts letter on her eleventh birthday. Book I - the Secret Library of Fire details the story of her first year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.</p><p>This story is a tribute to the world and magic of Harry Potter. It builds upon the universe created by J. K. Rowling but uses only original characters. References to the Harry Potter books appear explicitly but are also implicit in the world-building taken as precedent for the setting of this story.</p>
    </blockquote>
