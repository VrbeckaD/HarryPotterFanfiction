    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>He had done it. He had actually done it.<br/>The locket sat heavily in the palm of his hand as he collapsed against the sandy shore outside of the cave. His body ached where the inferi had grabbed hold of him and he shivered, remembering the feeling of death overwhelm him. If Kreacher had not apparated them out of the cave as fast as he had, Regulus was sure he’d be dead.</p>
<p>“Master Regulus,” The house elf spoke from beside him, sounding much more tired and weary than Regulus had ever heard before. “I must take you home.”</p>
<p>“No,” Regulus said a bit breathlessly. He couldn’t go back Grimmauld Place, knowing the locket would not be safe there. Not with all the Death Eaters that traveled in and out of it now that Orion was dead and Walburga nearly insane, unable to filter the guests that travelled in and out. He couldn’t go anywhere, not without anyone finding out what he had done. There was no safe space for him, no friends that he could trust, no family he could—</p>
<p>Family. Sirius.</p>
<p>“Kreacher,” He croaked, and the elf looked at him with concern. “I need you to take me to Sirius.”</p>
    </blockquote>
