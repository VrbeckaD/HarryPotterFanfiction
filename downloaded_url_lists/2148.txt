    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Being the eldest Malfoy comes with high expectations and more responsibility than you would like at 16 years old.</p>
<p>Added to the pressure is your impending ‘inheritance’ when you turn 17. As a Veela, you will go through changes during your inheritance that will make you more irresistible to your soulmate than ever before.</p>
<p>When the time comes, your parents expect your soulmate to a pureblood wizard like yourself. On the day of your 17th birthday, its revealed that your soulmate is a pureblood wizard.</p>
<p>However they wouldn’t approve…because your soulmate is a Weasley.</p>
<p>More specifically, your soulmate is Fred Weasley.</p>
<p>Your parents go beyond disapproving of your soulmate, they threaten you with complete disinheritance.</p>
<p>The choice is yours. You choose your soulmate or your family.</p>
<p>But you can’t have both.</p>
