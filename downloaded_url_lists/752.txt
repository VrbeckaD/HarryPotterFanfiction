    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>So I wanted this fic to represent the consent issues that both Harry and Draco have in this AU I've created. Consent is a hugely important thing in every situation and I wanted to write a scene in which one of them doesn't consent because in all the books and fics I've read I've never seen it happen. The start is left intentionally ambiguous so that I can finish it later. </p>
<p>It always ends up being a non-con, and I think it's important to show the other side of trauma that isn't people coercing others into something they aren't comfortable with. The non-con tag I've put is due to slight mentions of past non-con and violence. </p>
<p>With that knowledge in mind, go forth and read! I hope you enjoy this little ficlet I've made, and as always, kudos and comments are always appreciated :)</p>
    </blockquote>
