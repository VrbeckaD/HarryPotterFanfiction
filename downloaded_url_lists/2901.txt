    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Harry was looking forward to a nice quiet Christmas, and gets assigned to a case at the very last minute. He has to keep an eye on a smuggling ring, in the middle of nowhere, all alone. <p>Title inspired by Hailey Leithauser, who said in <a href="https://www.poetryfoundation.org/poetrymagazine/poems/53157/fever-56d232346b6f9" rel="nofollow">Fever</a>:</p>

<p>  <i>inside the placid</i><br/><i>scald</i></p>
<p>  <i>and hissing of the snow. </i></p>
<p> <br/>This fic is complete, I will be posting the other chapters in the next few days<br/>(lil spoiler in the end notes)</p>
