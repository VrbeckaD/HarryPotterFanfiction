      <p>Cassandra is a muggleborn witch with an interesting ability to witness a person's death before it happens. Will she learn to embrace and understand her unusual gift and save people she cares about, or will it all be for nothing?</p>

<p>“That they were going to die, professor. I knew. I saw it and I knew. But no one… nobody ever believed me.” </p>
<p>Professor Dumbledore was careful in keeping his expression calm. He didn’t want to let her know that this gift she spoke of was unusual and unheard of, though she probably already knew it. “Why, my dear girl, did you choose to let me know?”</p>
<p>Cassandra looked up at Dumbledore, a look of utter fear and despair etched across her face. “Because,” she paused, taking a deep breath and forcing herself to keep a steady gaze. “A war is coming, professor. One led by You-Know-Who.”</p>
