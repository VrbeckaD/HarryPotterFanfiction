He was certain that his appearance would have shocked people who had once known the self-confident, intelligent Slytherin prince and Malfoy heir. It was a façade that he had carefully maintained throughout his years at Hogwarts, even when his world was falling apart after he had joined Voldemort's ranks, but now he no longer cared enough to keep it up."</p>
