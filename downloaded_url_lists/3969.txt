    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>It's true, that Credence has not spoken since he was a child--except that he has. Not with his lips per se, but with his mind and through his late mother’s piano. His piano. The one that travels with him from his home in lush England to a new settlement community onto more foreign shores, after his grandfather’s decision to trade him off into the care of devout and strict Mary Lou Barebone. Thrown from one unloving and uncomfortable environment to another, Credence struggles to warm to his new life and surroundings, though he soon finds himself becoming slowly intrigued by the gruff Irishman who lives awhile away from them.</p>
