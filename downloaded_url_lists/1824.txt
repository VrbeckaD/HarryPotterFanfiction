      <p>And there he was. The one boy she could not stand. His messy, jet black hair was ruffled and untidy and his glasses were sitting crooked on his nose. James Potter was here. With Lily. And all her Muggle friends. At a Muggle ski resort. Could things possibly be worse? Oh yeah, Sirius Black was there, too.</p><p> <br/>*Originally written in 2009 and posted to MuggleNet FanFiction - with some slight edits*</p>
