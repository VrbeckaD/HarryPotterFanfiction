    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Mid-September, nine Beauxbatons students spontaneously appear in the middle of the Great Hall, amidst blood, smoke, and ritual fire. Upon investigation, the Hogwarts faculty and ministry aurors discover the one who transported the group is Vega Lang, one of the nine. A witch merely fifteen years old. A witch the ICW reported missing the last six years. A witch whose entire family perished in a serial spree of arson, which destroyed many of the most ancient Chinese families, the Langs being the first.</p><p>Death seems to follow the girl, some whisper. Only she and her siblings escaped death during that summer of genocide. Only she had the wherewithal to leave one location with anti-apparation wards, and appear immediately in another such location.</p><p>Who exactly is this girl? Is she a perpetrator or a victim? Prime Minister Hermione Granger refuses to believe Lang’s presence during both tragedies is coincidence, despite what members of the ICW claim. How could no one notice a missing child right under their noses for the last four years, albeit in another continent? Lang’s account is sparse; her extended family's and the ICW's even more so. The minister’s logic, and dare she say, gut, tells her none of it is coincidence.</p>
    </blockquote>
