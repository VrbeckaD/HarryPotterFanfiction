    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Power, connections and success: the Elites have it all. When Hermione Granger is denied the position of Class President- a position she needs for university- her place in society as an Ordinary is tragically defined. It does not help that the position was granted to her academic rival and childhood bully, Draco Malfoy. The two must work together if they wish to keep their positions, with rising expectations from Draco's family and Hermione's need to break the belief that she is "ordinary". However, a party and a debt bring about terrible circumstances for both Hermione and Draco. The two are forced to take drastic measures, but how far will they go?</p>
