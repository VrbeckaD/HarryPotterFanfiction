    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>What if Hogwarts didn't just have the Golden Trio? What if there was a Golden Triad?</p><p>Hermione Granger had always felt like something was missing from her life when she suddenly bumps into Fred and George Weasley at the Hogwarts Train in her first year and discovers that a soulbond ties them together. </p><p>She sets out to protect and save everyone she loves as she discovers the magical world is anything but perfect. Hermione goes out to prove that Sirius Black is innocent, attempts to save muggle-borns, keep Harry DEATH WISH Potter alive and keep her Triad a secret from those seeking to take the magic all while she is still in school.</p><p>Just a normal day for the brightest witch of her age.</p><p> </p><p>NOT ALL IN GRYFFINDOR, HOUSE DIVERSITY</p>
