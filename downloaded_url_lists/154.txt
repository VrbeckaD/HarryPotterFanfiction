    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Tom glanced through the window of the apothecary as he walked by it, but as Tom was about to continue, his eyes landed on a small figure, and his feet stopped automatically. </p><p>In the warm lighting of the store, a child dressed in a red furred jacket was sitting on the floor near the door. Chubby, rosy red cheeks were puffed out in a pout. Big emerald eyes glistened with angry tears, and the child’s ink black hair was disheveled. He looked to be around two or so years old. </p><p>There was an odd throb in chest at the sight, and Tom futilely tried to push down the wave of greed and possessiveness. The child was...adorable, as reluctant as he was to use the word.</p>
