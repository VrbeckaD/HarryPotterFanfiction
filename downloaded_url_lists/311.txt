    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Hogwarts finds an unconventional way to heal the scars of war, using the power of love and a crack in the wall.</p><p>
  <em>Officially, Lily Evans was taking a well-deserved break after completing her first day of class assignments. Unofficially, she had gone insane. Round the bend. Completely barmy. </em>
</p><p>
  <em>Because there was a crack in the wall of her dorm room, and it was taunting her.</em>
</p><p>
  <em>“Potter,” The fissure said, no louder than a whisper.</em>
</p><p>
  <em>She groaned, and rolled over on her bed, smooshing her face into the satin red pillow. Of course she wouldn’t be able to escape him, not even within the safety of her dorm.</em>
</p><p>
  <em>“Potter!” The rift said, a little louder.</em>
</p><p>
  <em>“Leave me alone!” Lily lifted her head to yell at the wall. “I’ve cracked,” She whispered, giggled, then groaned again. Against her brain’s wishes, her body got off the bed and knelt in front of the defect. “I don’t care how fit he got over the summer, stop talking to me about Potter!” She told the seam. Like a sane person.</em>
</p><p>
  <em>To make matters worse, the crack talked back.</em>
</p>
    </blockquote>
