    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Meet the Marauders, four restless schoolboys at Hogwarts who live for coming up with the best magical pranks, impressing girls and each other, and… playing in a rock band? There’s James, drummer and studious Quidditch Captain who can’t seem to let go of chasing the one girl who has no interest in him. There’s Remus, the soulful piano player who doesn’t find girls as interesting as his books of poetry and the literary heroes he worships. There’s Peter, the quick-witted if unassuming follower who has a knack for making all the right connections for their growing sound. And there’s Sirius, the cheerful rebel rocker whose greatest cause is to find, feel, and sing out the music, gathering guitars along the way and of course, girls. Join them on their summer and schooltime adventures as they navigate the joys and pitfalls of living out their teenage years during the 1970s and the rise of You Know Who. Interwoven with both era-specific and modern music lyrics and poetry. All Harry Potter characters and canon details belong to JK Rowling. I do not agree with certain views she has expressed in recent years, but I appreciate the Potterverse she has created for us to play in.</p>
