    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>In a blink, he had her crowded against the wall, standing a breath away. Lily shivered, but she didn't know if it was from the cold stone pressed against her back through her thin shirt, or the filthy way James’s mouth had tilted up as he took in the flush spreading across her cheeks and down her neck. </p>
<p>“Anger or fear?” he said quietly, leaning in so that his mouth brushed against hers. </p>
<p>“Anger,” she said, and she closed her eyes as she heard the way her voice shook unconvincingly. </p>
<p>“Are you sure?” The smirk was evident in his words. Oh, she wanted to smack that smirk off his face. She hated that he knew her so well, had caught her so easily. </p>
<p>She hated that she liked being here, pressed against the wall by James Potter, breathing the same breath as him, anticipating his touch on her skin. </p>
<p>“No.”</p>
