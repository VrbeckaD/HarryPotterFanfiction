      <p>The untimely fate of the universe at large shifts when three Chosen Ones are given the opportunity to travel back in time and rewrite their history their way. In response, the fate of the universe shifts for the better. </p><p>As far as Hari Peverell, Loki Silvertounge, and Anakin Skywalker care, their soulmates are now of paramount importance. Their only actual plans™ are; get revenge, reconnect with their trusted, have fun, and fuck shit up. The wild adventures they drag each other on as they fight to save the galaxy together are honestly just an added bonus. Somehow amongst all the chaos of (mostly) reliving their lives they manage to have fun together.</p><p>(The Story skips around until; The Phantom Menace &amp; The Philosopher's Stone)</p>
