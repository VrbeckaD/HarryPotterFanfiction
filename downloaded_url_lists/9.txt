    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>Harry Potter has been offered the job of being Hogwarts's Defense Against the Dark Arts Professor every single year since the  year after he graduated from Hogwarts. He always felt like Hogwarts was his real home, but after the war everything just seemed to be tied to some kind terrible memory. Harry was sure he would never take the job, he didn't think he could handle going back there again, but when his oldest son gets his Hogwarts letter he changes his mind for no particular reason. It's not because he's paranoid, nope, it totally isn't. He just finally realized it would be a good job for him despite the memories it might bring to the surface. </p><p>James Sirius had no idea what to expect in his first year at Hogwarts. His parents both had very unusual years there and therefore he didn't really have much to go off of for a normal first year. Then again, maybe his family is a bit cursed in the "normal" department, because a few weeks after school started kids started going missing, with no clues left behind as to who did it, or how they did it.</p>
    </blockquote>
