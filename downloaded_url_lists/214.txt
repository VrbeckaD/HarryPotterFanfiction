    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>MAJOR TRIGGER WARNING <br/>The events leading up to the halfblood prince breaks Draco's soul. He fights to breathe everyday and struggles to find purpose and belonging. He finds that he could care less about everything now.<br/>MAJOR TRIGGER WARNING </p>
<p> I scoot closer to the edge of the terrace looking over the beautiful terrain that is Malfoy Manor knowing that one single slip up would cost me my life but I just can’t bring myself to care anymore. When I was a child I would hate this place because of how high it was off the ground and would always fear I would fall off even knowing that as long as I was behind the railings I would be safe but lately… I don’t care. I feel blissfully empty as I stare down at the ground as questions swirl through my head. One by one they consume my thoughts.</p>
