    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>You didn't know where you were going. Just that you wanted—needed—to get away. Not forever, you thought. Just for the night. But there were nights it all became too much, and the curiosity and loneliness exploded out of you, and you left. You went down the path along the Forest.</p><p>Only to the edge. That was the plan, and it came to you at some point when your hand was leaving a trail of blood on the crunchy leaves, your heart beating heavily in your chest.
