      <p>He's been exiled from the Wizarding World, the Wizengamot's cruel way of giving him the second chance that Hermione Granger insisted he receive for his crucial role that led to their victory. All the while she researches the means to undo the Obliviation without success.<br/>Following the advice of Snape's portrait, she goes to see Narcissa Malfoy, wondering what she will learn.<br/>He gets some unexpected help from a kind couple after losing everything in rapid succession.<br/>They give him a place to stay, a job, and teach him their muggle ways.<br/>And their last name is Wilkins.</p>
