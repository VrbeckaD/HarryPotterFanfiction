    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>We all know the story of 'The Boy Who Lived'. Every child knows his name. Everyone loves the tale of how Harry Potter saved the Wizarding World from Voldemort.</p><p>But do we know the story of 'The Greatest Sorcerer Ever To Walk The Earth'? Every time Ron cried, <em>"Merlin's beard!", </em>did we stop to wonder who exactly Merlin was, and what he had done to be recognized worldwide and have an order named after him?</p><p>
  <em>This is the story of a young boy, who thought that he was a nobody, growing into a man, who would be remembered for centuries.</em>
</p>
