      <p>Newt Harper is a fifth year Hufflepuff just about to start their OWL year in Hogwarts. Like many others in their year, Newt holds assumptions about their Slytherin Classmates. <p>* Based this on my own experiences with gender and although I do not identify as trans, I certainly have had a long process exploring gender. I hope this conveys. </p>
<p>ALT. UNIVERSE</p>
<p>So, this story takes place in a universe where Voldemort was not involved in Harry's schooling years. See below for the differences. </p>
<p>YEAR ONE: Quirrell attempted to steal the stone for his own gain, he was caught by Harry in the same manner as canon but was not killed. Instead he was fired. </p>
<p>YEAR TWO: The Chamber of Secrets was opened and Riddle's Diary is the same. However, it was the final horcrux so when it was destroyed so was Voldemort. </p>
<p>YEAR THREE: The same as canon, however Pettigrew was caught and Sirius is free. </p>
<p>YEAR FOUR: Cedric is the only Hogwarts Champion and he won.</p>
