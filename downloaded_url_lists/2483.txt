    <h6 class="landmark heading">Summary</h6>
    <blockquote class="userstuff summary">
      <p>“Sirius Black escapes from Azkaban.” </p><p>Harry read the words out loud. His features were lit up with understanding as he raised his head to look at his dad, whose eyes had filled with tears upon Harry’s words making the headline real. Remus wasn’t dreaming, he hadn’t misread. Like the text upon a gravestone, signalling the reality of death, Harry’s utterance of the words had made them real. Confirmed their meaning. </p><p>Sirius Black had escaped from Azkaban. <br/>---<br/>Third book of a series. Harry has been raised with love and care by Remus Lupin, resulting in major changes in the Harry Potter book series.</p>
