      <p>The cruelest thing Remus could ever tell Harry was that he should never love Snape.</p>
<p>He’s seen those hungry emerald eyes staring at the professor, whether it was in the hallways or at Order meetings. <p>All of this, Remus could understand, because he too had suffered from the same dilemma.</p>
