          <div class="userstuff"><p>
  <span>Harry latched himself on to Tom, he refused to look at either of the friends that he left behind, that he hadn’t chosen. </p><p>
  <span>“It’s okay baby, I’ll keep you safe. Such a good boy.” Tom whispered into Harry’s ear, as if it would muffle the sound of his friends screaming.</span>
</p><p>
