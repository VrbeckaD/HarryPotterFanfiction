    <p>
  <em>
    <span>"I mark the hours, every one, Nor have I yet outrun the Sun. My use and value, unto you, Are gauged by what you have to do</span>
  </em>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>Remember the plan, if I fail, you go back in time and reset it, tell me this is a terrible idea Harry said</p>
<p>
  <span>This is a terrible idea! Hermione said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Ok, just remind me again then</span>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>Hermione watched as Harry and Voldemort faced off. Green and red shot out of their wands. There was a huge explosion. And in the end, they both died.</p>
<p>
  <span>No! No! NO! Harry! Hermione yelled</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>They had both fallen. Both sides started fighting again. Hermione picked up the time turner and spun it. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione look out! Luna yelled</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Luna ran into Hermione to push her out of the way. She threw up a shield to block whatever spell was being thrown. The spell shattered the shield and hit them both, knocking them to the ground. </span>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>The two awoke eventually. They sat up and saw they were in the courtyard of Hogwarts, except it was different. It looked as if there was no battle fought. They two of them got up and looked around. Hermione saw the pieces of the time turner on the ground and picked them up.</p>
<p>
  <span>Is that a time turner? Luna asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Yes, but I dont know how far back weve gone Hermione said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>You two! What are you doing no in uniform and not in class?! a voice boomed from behind them</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>The two turned around and saw a dark haired student, with a shiny head boy badge on his robes. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Who are you? Luna asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Im head boy, and I want to know what you two are doing out here. I should take points from both of you! </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Youre not head boy Hermione said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I believe this says that I am the boy pointed to his badge</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Riddle don't you have better things to do than to harass these lovely ladies? another voice said from behind</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione and Luna saw 3 more boys in school uniforms appear behind the dark haired boy. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Riddle? Youre Tom Riddle? Hermione asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Yes, now who are you two? Tom asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione gripped her wand tighter in her hand and was about to hex him, when Luna placed her hand on her arm.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Were new here, were looking for the headmaster Luna said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Luna, just let me-</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Its alright, our thestral carriage arrived early. The headmaster is expecting us Luna said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Oh new students? </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Let us escort you</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Malfoy, Nott! You act as if youve never seen girls before Riddle said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermiones eyes dashed over to the blonde standing behind Riddle. A Malfoy, He resembled a lot like Draco, the same hair, eyes and pointy features. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I dont tolerate lying Riddle said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>You dont seem to tolerate a lot of things it seems Luna said</span>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>
  <span>Hermione and Luna m</span>
  <span>ade their way to Dumbledores office. He was much younger and looked at them strangely.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>We were not expecting guests Dumbledore said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Were not guests, were students Luna said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>We have a lot to tell you Hermione said showing him the broken time turner</span>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>Hermione and Luna walked through the hallways. Dumbledore agreed to let them stay but told them they could not alter the past, no matter how much they wanted it to. The consequences could be dire he said. Hermione was determined to kill Riddle. Luna placed her hand Hermiones shoulder.</p>
<p>
  <span>I know its hard for you, but we cant change things Luna said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I dont care. Ill do anything to save him</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I know you cared for him deeply</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>How-</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I notice a lot of things people dont see. I know he meant a lot to you, but you cant kill Riddle.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>It could stop the war from happening. Harry would still have his parents, Neville could have his parents back, Luna think of all the good it would do</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>But it would weigh heavy on your soul. And right now hes just a teenager, like us</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>No, by now hes already made his first horcrux, hell make more. Hes already killed people. Hes opened the chamber. He has to be stopped</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Alright, we can stop him, but we cant kill him. I know you miss him. But he wouldnt want you to do this</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hes dead, no one knows what he wants</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>What are you two doing out in the halls? Riddle said from behind</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Luna turned around and saw him standing there, looking angry again. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>You always look angry. Is there something wrong? Luna asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Yes, you two arent in your rooms where you belong Riddle said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>We were on our way, youre merely delaying us Luna said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Off you go, both of you! Riddle said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Luna shrugged and skipped off into the hallway. Riddle watched her, confused that she was skipping. Hermione turned and walked away. </span>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>The next morning in the great hall, Luna and Hermione were eating breakfast. Hermione glanced over to the Slytherin table and saw Riddle there with Malfoy, Nott and LeStrange. Of course he would be with the purebloods. She wondered if they knew that Riddle was a halfblood.</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione, stop staring, were trying to blend in remember? Luna said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I cant help it. Hes just sitting there so smug, when hes going to destroy the world, Hes going to kill Harry</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>He doesnt done it yet, hes just a boy right now</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Who will turn into a monster</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>What if we oblivate him</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>What?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>You know erase his memories, so hes not hell bent on destroying the world, maybe he just wants to belong, is that so wrong?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>How can you say that?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Because I know how that feels like. And what if he just wants to be loved?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hes incapable of love. Hes a product of a love potion. His mother used it on his father. She released him from it and he ran away. He cant love</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>How sad</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Right, Im sure all he needs is a hug</span>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>In potions class Slughorn was lecturing while Hermione glanced over and saw Malfoy and Riddle talking. She wasnt sure which Malfoy this was. Was it Luciuss father? Or grandfather? Hermione got up to get the ingredients from the cupboard. She felt someone brush past her and she looked over. Malfoy was standing next to her, dangerously close to her.</p>
<p>
  <span>Im Malfoy, Abraxas Malfoy</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>From the Greek god Hermes. Daughter of the King of Sparta, Menelaus and Helen</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Yes</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Are you the daughter of a king?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Im not a pureblood if thats what youre asking</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Half blood?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione pulled up her sleeve to show off her scarred arm. Abraxas eyes widened at jagged skin on her arm. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>That answer you question? Hermione said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Who did that to you?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>A crazy blood purist. Shes dead</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I would hope so</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Too bad she didnt die before she did this to me</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione turned and left. Abraxas gathered up the ingredients and returned to the table. </span>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>Luna was walking to the library when she saw Hermione and Abraxas arguing.</p>
<p>
  <span>You bushy haired little swot! Abraxas yelled</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Pointy faced ferret!</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Ferret?!</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Really? Thats all youre concerned about? Youre such a stuck up little prat</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Nosy little know it all bint!</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Inbred blood purist git!</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Inbred?!</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Im surprised your inbreeding didnt cause you all to die out! Or have extra limbs and be deformed</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Enraged Abraxas grabbed Hermione and kissed her. Hermione froze for a second before kissing him back. Luna looked at the two surprised for a moment and then smiled. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>It was bound to happen: Luna said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione pushed Abraxas away and looked at him horrified. She ran off down the hallways. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Are they going to be like that now? I do hate all this drama and angst Riddle said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Most likely, theyre very explosive together Luna said</span>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>Luna was feeding the thestrals when she saw Riddle walking nearby. The thestrals scattered and she was left standing alone. He watched her for a moment, watching her trying to coax them back out. She was barefoot on the grass and her hair was freely hanging loose.</p>
<p>
  <span>Theres nothing to be frightened of, we can see you now Luna said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Riddle watched as the thestrals came back to her. Riddle had never seen them before until he killed Myrtle and his father to create the horcruxes. </span>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>Hermione was walking through the halls, when she saw Luna hugging Riddle. Riddle looked uncomfortable, refusing to hug her back.</p>
<p>
  <span>Release me at once Riddle said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Someone said all you needed was a hug Luna said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Well they were wrong very very wrong</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>They say human contact, especially hugs makes people more happy. And happy people dont look angry all the time like you</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I am not angry all the time!</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Your face says otherwise</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>If you hug me back Ill release you</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Fine Riddle said as he awkwardly placed his arms around her. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>See, doesnt that feel better?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>No particularly</span>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>Abraxas followed Hermione into the room of requirement. He was surprised she knew it existed. She was inside what appeared to be 12 Grimmauld Place. Hermione sat down on the chair, thinking at any moment Harry would run through the door, but he wouldnt because he was dead. She saw Abraxas standing there.</p>
<p>
  <span>How did you get in here?! Hermione asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I followed you</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Well you can get out now</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>No, I want to know why you kissed me back</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I didnt want to</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>But you did</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>It was an accident!</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Theres no accidental kissing!</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Just get out!</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Not until you answer me!</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>No!</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Abraxas grabbed her and kissed her again. Hermione pushed him away.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Stop kissing me! Hermione said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Fine! Abraxas said as he let her go</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione grabbed onto his shirt and pulled him back and kissed him. </span>
</p>
<hr/>
<p>Hermione sat up and looked around. Her clothes were scattered on the floor and next to her lay a very naked Abraxas. She summoned her clothes to her and started to get dressed. This wasnt supposed to happen. This was Dracos grandfather! She buttoned up her shirt whens he felt his hands on her shoulder. He was behind her pushing her hair aside. She looked at his long pale hands. She reminded her of Draco. But he wasn't Draco. Draco wasnt even born yet, and she had to preserve the timeline. Abraxas had to have Lucius, and Lucius would have Draco. All thoughts always went back to Draco.</p>
<p>
  <span>Leaving so soon? Abraxas asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Its late, and I should go</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>You should, but you shouldnt leave yet, not without this</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione turned around and saw Abraxas holding a ring. A very familiar ring. It was the ring Draco gave her. His ring, with the M insignia on it. Hermione reached out for it only to have Abraxas pull his hand away.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I want to know how you got this considering Im wearing the only one Abraxas said as he turned his hand into a fist and turned it around showing the ring he was wearing. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I got it from Draco</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Who the hell is that?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hes, hes your grandson, and I loved him as much as anyone could love a person</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Absolute rubbish! This is obviously a cheap gemino charm</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Its not and you know it. There are protective spells and charms on this ring. It cannot be duplicated, only passed down. It offers the wearer protection against hexes and jinxes and to freely enter the manor if they wish. It allows them to move past the blood wards if necessary</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>How do you know this?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>I know because Draco gave it to me. Its how I was able to escape the mad woman who did this to my arm. Do you wish to see my memories?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Yes, if what you say is true, then what are you doing here? In the past?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>To save him, in my time, Draco dies in the war</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Theres a war?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Yes, the second wizarding war. He dies fighting a dark wizard</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Who?</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>In my time hes known as Voldemort, in your time you know him as Tom Riddle</span>
</p>
