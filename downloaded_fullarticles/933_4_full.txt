      <a href="/works/12826929/chapters/29284530">Chapter 1</a>: Presents

  <div class="userstuff module" role="article">
    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p> </p><p>
  <em>Once upon a time, before I came, you were all alone. You cried and cried and watched TV all day and were like a zombie. But you wished and wished for me, and Light flickered and I came shooting out onto rug, NYOOM. Then you cutted the cord and said 'Hello Scorpius.'</em>
</p><p> </p><p>I blink my eyes open. Today is a special day. It's my birthday and I'm five. Before today I was four, then a bit of time ago I was three then two then one then zero. Five is the biggest I've ever been.</p><p> </p><p>"Pa, wake up!" I nudge him. He groans and slowly opens his eyes. "Pa, I'm five!" He wraps his arms around me and rolls me up in a hug. "Happy birthday, Scorp." He whispers into my hair, white like his. Pa insists that its white-blond, but it's really just white.</p><p> </p><p>"Pa," I ask. "was I negative before zero?"</p><p> </p><p>Pa laughs. "No, you weren't born before you were zero."</p><p> </p><p>"Where was I before then?" I ask, pulling off my sleep top and pulling on a pair of pants. Pa shrugs.</p><p> </p><p>"You were in the Sky." Pa says vaguely. He means outer space.</p><p> </p><p>"And then you wished and wished for me, and I came crashing down through Light onto rug?"</p><p> </p><p>Pa laughs again. "Something like that, Scorp."</p><p> </p><p>Pa gets breakfast ready whilst I say hello to Room. I say hello to Eggsnake, Wardrobe, Sink, to TV. I have to say hello to Room otherwise it won't know to be awake and it might sleep through my birthday.</p><p> </p><p>"It's my birthday!" I whisper to Eggsnake. He's my favourite.  "Pa, it's my birthday. I'm five."</p><p> </p><p>Pa smiles at me as he ties his long hair back. My hair goes down my back all the way to my butt and Pa's goes down a little way past his shoulders.  "So, Mr.Five, what would you like to do first, eat breakfast or find out what special activity we're doing today?"</p><p> </p><p>I chew on my finger as I decide. I choose breakfast first.</p><p> </p><p>I have to take a vitamin before everything. Pa says they'll keep me strong. He doesn't have vitamins, he says he doesn't need one, and he's bigger than me so I think he's strong anyway. Then we have breakfast. It's cornflakes. Pa pours both of ours to make sure I get the right amount, I'm rubbish at counting but Pa used to say it's because I'm only four. But now I'm five, I might be better at it. Halfway through he starts rubbing the outside of his mouth. "Is Bad Tooth hurting?" I ask. He nods and taps his head.</p><p> </p><p>"There are worse things." He reminds me.</p><p> </p><p>"Like your bad wrist?" I ask. Pa nods.</p><p> </p><p>"Just like my bad wrist."</p><p> </p><p>I finish my breakfast extra quick because I want to find out what Pa has planned. Last year on my birthday I got a thing called a sugar mouse, and it took me all day to eat it.</p><p> </p><p>"Mr Five, how do you feel about baking a birthday cake?" Pa asks.</p><p> </p><p>My mouth drops open.</p><p> </p><p>"A birthday cake? Like in TV?" I say, not really believing him. I didn't even know they existed in Room. I thought it was just a TV thing or a story thing, like dogs and dragons and pigeons.</p><p> </p><p>"Just like TV, but real."</p><p> </p><p>I grin toothily. "No way."</p><p> </p><p>Pa laughs. Before we have birthday cake, we have to exercise first. Pa says I'm the best at stretch and running, but I think he's lying because he can stretch more than me, even though I can almost get both legs to go in completely opposite directions. I'm super better at running. Pa doesn't do running because he's too tall and it only takes him one big step to get from one end of room to the other.</p><p> </p><p>I jump up and down on Bed as Pa puts Table back where it should be on top of Rug.</p><p> </p><p>"I'm Scorpius the dragon slayer! I'm gonna burst out of Light and with my dragon and bong bong bong with all of the planets and dragons!"</p><p> </p><p>Making a cake is messy and fun. The flour goes everywhere, mostly because we threw some at each other when I got bored. Pa lets me do the stirring because of his bad wrist, his left one is all red and bruised a bit below the tattoo and around the bracelet. We have to be careful not to crack the eggshells too bad, Eggsnake needs them to grow. When it's baking I wash up and Pa sleeps. He sleeps a lot more than I do, even though I'm smaller. He says it's because he gets less energy from sleep, so he has to do it more.</p><p> </p><p>I watch TV while the cake is baking. Teletubbies is on, and Po is my favourite, because it sounds like Pa. I watch it until Pa pulls back the covers in bed and then quickly turn it off, wanting to taste the cake.</p><p> </p><p>The cake is done in the next five minutes and Pa makes me wait at Table while he puts the icing and candles and fire on. I close my eyes while he puts it down.</p><p> </p><p>The cake is as big as the plate, and the icing is white and has 5, the number way not the letter way, on it. There's no candles and no fire. I don't understand. It can't be a birthday cake without them.</p><p> </p><p>"Now with the candles and fire?" I ask, confused.</p><p> </p><p>Pa makes a face. Pa says, tapping his bad wrist. I nod. Being sick made Room smell for <em>weeks</em>.</p><p> </p><p>Pa and me play mermen in Bath, like we did on my birthday. We end up staying the bath longer than usual because the numbers on Watch are smaller than they usually are which means we have more minutes before They come. Pa says that they come later on Monday's so it would've been fine anyway. That's another one of his weird names for time.</p><p> </p><p>Because we have so much time we decide to make Eggsnake grow using the eggs from my birthday. TV is showing a grainy picture because the signal isn't very good today. Neither of us actually watch it, it's just noise in the background.</p><p> </p><p>"Pa, where do we go when we're asleep?"</p><p> </p><p>"Right here in Room."</p><p> </p><p>"But dreams; do we go into TV for dreaming?" My eyes flick to the grainy picture and then back at Pa's face.</p><p> </p><p>"We're never anywhere but here." Pa squints as he pushes the needle through one of Eggsnake's shells.</p><p> </p><p>"Can you have a nightmare if you're awake?" I ask. Pa shrugs.</p><p> </p><p>"I guess things can feel like a nightmare, but not really, no."</p><p> </p><p>I guess Old Tom and Red aren't nightmares, then. Besides, They bring us Sundaytreat so how bad can They be?</p><p> </p><p>We put Eggsnake under Bed when we're done. Eggsnake does a lot of hibernating. Pa lifts up his top and does the spell. I have lots from the right. Pa tells me another story, tonight from Tales of Beedle the Bard.</p><p> </p><p>I'm wide awake when Door goes <em>beep beep beep</em>. It's just Old Tom today. He has a bag that he hands to Pa too.</p><p> </p><p>"What's that smell?" Old Tom asks.</p><p> </p><p>"I burnt some cheese earlier. Sorry, I just wasn't thinking." Pa does the same thing he does with Red; turns away and puts the things Old Tom brought in from space away in Fridge or Shelf.</p><p> </p><p>Old Tom laughs cruelly. "Thinking isn't your strong point."</p><p> </p><p>Pa makes a small noise back and I'm not sure if he's agreeing or not. I hope he's not; Pa is the bestest thinker ever.</p><p> </p><p>"The vitamins?" He asks suddenly.</p><p> </p><p>Old Tom does a sigh and a nodding thing. I think it's sarcasm but silent. "There's nothing in them."</p><p> </p><p>Pa sighs. "Maybe if we had a better diet." He mutters, except not really because I can hear him.</p><p> </p><p>"Fuck, here we go again. Maybe if you could stop complaining, and be a little grateful for what you have."</p><p> </p><p>Pa goes all stiff over the counter. "Thank you... for everything." He says in a whisper voice, quickly turning around and then looking away again.</p><p> </p><p>"Yeah, and what do you go and do? Raise a goddamn kleptomaniac." I don't know what that means, but he sounds so <em>angry</em>.</p><p> </p><p>"What?" Pa's voice is quieter than a whisper.</p><p> </p><p>"See!" Old Tom waggles his finger at Pa. "You can't even control the little shite. Ruby offered him something but he politely declined, and guess what was missing this afternoon?"</p><p> </p><p>Who's <em>Ruby</em>? And what is he-</p><p> </p><p>
  <em>Oh</em>
</p><p> </p><p>The <em>lollipop</em>! I reach under Wardrobe's pillow and pull it out, still in its wrapper. Maybe if I gave it back he wouldn't be so mad. . .</p><p> </p><p>"So I think it's time the little shite learned a lesson." Old Tom stands suddenly and Pa jumps. He turns and stares straight at me. I want to hide under Blanket, but I can't move.</p><p> </p><p>"No! Not him, you have me!" Pa says in a wobbly, almost shouty voice. Old Tom takes another step towards me, almost reaching Wardrobe's doors.</p><p> </p><p>"No!" Pa yells. He pushes Old Tom away from Wardrobe and stands in front of it, blocking my view.</p><p> </p><p>"The fuck did you just-!" Old Tom roars. I gasp, but they don't hear me because Old Tom grabs Pa and throws him from in front of Wardrobe.</p><p> </p><p>There's a thud, and then another and then Bed starts creaking, faster and louder than it ever has before. Someone makes a choking sound.</p><p> </p><p>"Do you want to fucking breathe?" Old Tom yells.</p><p> </p><p>"Don't - Touch. . ." Pa voice sounds all croaky and scratchy and horrible and painful.</p><p> </p><p>"Do you wanna breathe?!" Old Tom yells.</p><p> </p><p>Someone makes a weird noise. I think it's Pa; it has to be because Old Tom is still yelling.</p><p> </p><p>"Then shut the fuck up!"</p><p> </p><p>Someone takes in a huge breath. "Don't touch him, don't touch him!" Pa repeats.</p><p> </p><p>"Ever grab me like that, I'll fucking kill you. And your brat."</p><p> </p><p>"Don't touch him!" Pa's voice sounds all horrible and raspy and <em>it's all my fault</em>.</p><p> </p><p>"Why don't you think about how you got him?" I don't know what Old Tom means by that, but then Door goes beep beep beep and he's gone.</p><p> </p><p>"Scorp? Scorpius?" Pa croaks.</p><p> </p><p>I run out of Wardrobe and onto Bed, Pa wraps his arms around me and holds me close. Lamp is on and Pa's eyes are red and his neck has dark patches and his pants are at his knees. He yanks them up with one arm and holds me tightly with the other.</p><p> </p><p>Pa rocks me back and forth and suddenly Room goes black. I gasp in Pa's arms.</p><p> </p><p>"Pa, what's happening?" I hear him say a bad word.</p><p> </p><p>"They. . . I think They cut the power." Pa gets out of Bed and I hear Wardrobe open. He gets out my warm clothes and we both put them on. I hear a thud and freeze, thinking it's Them, but it's just Pa trying to open Door. Dumbo Pa. We can't open Door, only They can. "What do we do?"</p><p> </p><p>Pa slides into Bed next to be, pulling me close again. I wrap my arms and legs around him and press my face into his hair. "Just sleep, Scorp." I don't want to sleep but I'm tired and -</p>
