<p>'oh,' said zabini, sounding startled. 'who's that?'</p>
<p>'that's bill weasley.' parvati couldn't exactly strangle him at this point in the evening, so she settled for tearing the roll with her teeth.</p>
<p>zabini's lips did something funny. 'he's very--big.'</p>
<p>'he's married,' parvati said, so loudly that bill saw them and came over.</p>
<p>'parv the marv!' bill cheered. 'didn't expect to see you out tonight. and who's this?'</p>
<p>'hullo. my name's bl-blaire,' said zabini, fumblingly, if pleasantly. his face looked so different, under the polyjuice. clearly he'd forgot - he blinked very slowly. 'who are you?' as if parvati hadn't just told him.</p>
<p>'i'm--' said bill, blinking back in confused silence. zabini grinned a little. sweetly, even. parvati was going to sick up everywhere, and never stop.</p>
<p>'married,' parvati mumbled through her mouthful of bread.</p>
<p>'--married,' bill agreed, smiling a little, rueful.</p>
<p>'well, hullo, married,' said zabini, tilting his head. ugh, he was <em>cheeky</em>. bill loved that kind of thing. 'pleasure to make your acquaintance.'</p></div>
