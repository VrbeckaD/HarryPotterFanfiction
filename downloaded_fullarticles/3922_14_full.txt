        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p>Entering the dungeons, he swallows the last piece of the bread he brought with him from dinner. Ends up with crumbles on his shirt and earning disgusted looks from passing Slytherins, no doubt wondering what he is doing down here after hours.</p>
<p>Brushing the crumbs off of him with one hand, he knocks the door of the potions classroom with the other. ”Sir, Harry Potter reporting for detention sir.”</p>
<p>”What did you do this time, <em>Potter</em>?” is the reply from inside. Harry enters and, as he closes the door behind him, Malfoy looks up. He stands before a large cauldron, stirring the contents with his wand. ”Hm?”</p>
<p>”Nothing, honestly. Snape just can't miss an opportunity to get alone time with me – it's sad, really. He's starting to look desperate. Where is he?”</p>
<p>”How should I know, I'm not his keeper.”</p>
<p>As he passes the table Malfoy is working by, there's something about the scent of the potion – it seems to belong somewhere else, a scent not associated with any potion he can think of. Before he can place it though, Malfoy notices him staring, and goes ”<em>What</em>, Potter?”</p>
<p>”Nothing.” He strolls towards the back of the classroom, hands in his pockets. ”What are you doing here, anyway? Earned yourself the wrath of the potions master? Land your scrawny arse in detention?</p>
