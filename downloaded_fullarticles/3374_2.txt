<p><i>The spell was purple. It flew swiftly and steadily. There was no pain, only release. All anguish ended and he fell into sweet oblivion.<br/></i><br/>Rabastan cried out, rushed forward. Eyes turned towards him, a few responding shouts sounded. He didn’t see the Auror step forward in his rush towards Potter; didn’t see him raise his wand.</p>
<p>The spell was purple. It flew swiftly and steadily. There was no pain, only release. All anguish ended and he fell into sweet oblivion. Everything ended that day – all pain and all hope – <i>everything</i>.</p></div>
