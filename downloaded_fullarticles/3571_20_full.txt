  <span>“DRACO ABRAXUS MALFOY, YOU GET YOUR ARSE BACK HERE RIGHT NOW!” roared a very angry female voice just as a cackling blonde ran past a group of first year hufflepuffs who nearly fainted at the sight of the head slytherin looking so happy.</span>

  </b>

</p>
<p>

<p>
  <span>“It’s the weekend, Hermione, no one is trying to study, and if they are they probably enjoyed the little show they just got.” Ron grumbled. </span>

  <span>Hermione glared at Ron. He cowered and took another bite of his sandwich. She turned her glare on Harry who suddenly looked sheepish. “So maybe he’s doing a bit much. I’ll tell him to stay out of the library.</span>

<p><span>“I’ve got it!” he announced, looking immensely pleased with himself. </span><span><br/></span> <span>“Well, then, what is it?” Harry smiled.</span></p>
<p>

<p>

<p>

<p>
  <span>“Potter!” </span>

  <span>Harry grumbled but was shook again by the persistent blonde. “Potter!”</span>

<p>
  <span>“What, Draco? I’m trying to sleep.” </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Nevermind that, look! I made my first balloon animal!” he beamed. Draco looked so happy that Harry couldn’t help but smile groggily.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Oh yeah?” he said followed quickly by a yawn.</span>

<p>

<p>

Either way his heart nearly broke at the teary look in his boyfriends grey eyes. </span>

Harry felt a rush of adoration at the stubborn, childish look on his face as he held Rupert carefully in his arms. </span>
