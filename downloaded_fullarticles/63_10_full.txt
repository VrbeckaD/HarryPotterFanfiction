  <span>Draco stuffed his hands into his pockets and walked up to stand next to her. A somewhat elderly vagabond was laying on the floor with his throat slashed. Although blood wasn’t exactly </span>
  <em>
    <span>gushing</span>
  </em>
  <span>, there was still a steady stream that had stained the carpet and the hardwood floor. Draco turned to Hermione and pursed his lips.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Two?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Three,” Hermione corrected and sighed. “And you made a mess.”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I was in a rush,” Draco explained as he wrapped his arms around her torso. “I cleaned myself up first, coming back for him, but the magic… It came on quickly.” </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione had had a feeling that was what their latest escapade had been due to. The days after the curse's fulfillment were often felt in waves. Effects that she could feel washing over her as Draco stood behind her, a hand on her hip and the other creeping its way up to her breasts.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Eight years. Eight deaths. If the Ministry had known that this would be the result, that marriage law would have never been passed.</span>
</p>
