    <p>"Let me see if I have this correct. For the past eight months, Professor Snape has been pretending to be my father. What started at the end of October. He had fought for months not believing the potions were true. He searched for months trying to find out how it was done. He had told Weasley and Granger that something was off. They didn't believe him. No longer." </p><p>"Oh, poor little boy, what's a matter, your relatives won't bow to your whims now that you have my last name? His face was set in stone, his posture stiff and as he opened his mouth, in the coldest voice possible. "You know nothing.
