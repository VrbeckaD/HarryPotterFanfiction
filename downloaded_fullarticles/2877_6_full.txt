    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p>After the war, Draco couldnt sleep in the Manor. The bedrooms held too many nightmares. When he laid down for the night, squeezing his eyes closed against the darkness, he felt trapped, suffocated. He felt like he would die.</p>
<p>He took to sleeping outside, which baffled his mother and annoyed his father, but only for a little while. There would be no trials for Draco and his mother, thanks to Potter and his testimony, but his father could not be saved. Draco didnt know how he felt. </p>
<p>There was something so peaceful about the nighttime sky. The Manor was surrounded by miles of rural farmland, so the stars were quite bright. Draco liked looking at them. He liked staring into the black sky, contemplating a world beyond his own, a world of emptiness and nothingness. Sometimes, as his eyes fluttered near sleep, he imagined floating up up until he was swallowed.</p>
<p>Would he ever see his father again? Could he even stomach it?</p>
<p></p><center>
  <p>*</p>
</center>Life must go on.<p>Draco and his mother were picking through their garden. All of their house-elves had run away, so their veg garden was their main source of food. </p>
<p>What are you trying to tell me? He wiped the sweat from his forehead and cast another shielding charm. The day was hot and humid, and he wished the sun would just piss off. </p>
<p>You must go back to school.</p>
<p>No.</p>
<p>Look around you. Our world has changed. You will need an education. You will need to be employed.</p>
<p>He knew what his father would say. <i>Deep down shes still a Black. She doesnt understand the Malfoy ways.</i> Hed always brought up his mothers family when he planned to do something stupid and she didnt agree. </p>
<p>What about you? What are you going to do when Im at Hogwarts?</p>
<p>She smiled weakly. Dont worry about me. Ill be all right.</p>
<p>You will be awfully lonely.</p>
<p>She shrugged. It will be good to live peacefully.</p>
<p>I cant leave you.</p>
<p>Draco.</p>
<p>He turned away from her. Im not going back.</p>
<p>Draco.</p>
<p>No.</p>
<p>If you dont go now, you will be alienated for the rest of your life.</p>
<p>I dont care.</p>
<p>You are too young to understand how hard it is to live.</p>
<p>He laughed, and the sound was hollow. I know what its like to not want to live.</p>
<p>It gets worse. Much worse. Please, do it for me. I want you to be happy.</p>
<p>Mother, please.</p>
<p>No, listen. I love you too much to let you throw away your life. I will be fine. Go back to school. Make new friends and alliances. You will need them.</p>
<p>He thought about facing everyone hed betrayed. He thought about roaming the corridors without Vincent. He thought about seeing Potter again. </p>
<p>I cant go back.</p>
<p>His mother gripped his shoulder, her voice firm. You will.</p>
<p></p><center>
  <p>*</p>
