    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p>Ebony robes flowed behind their wearer as he walked gracefully down the corridors of Hogwarts.</p><p>Just then, Severus heard something so beautiful it made him turn toward the sound.</p><p>It seemed to be coming from farther down the corridor.</p><p>It was the soprano voice of a girl singing. Most likely, a student.</p><p>Severus scowled.</p><p>He listened closely to follow the song and find the singer.</p><p>The Potions Master wished to give her detention for disturbing the silence he revelled in.</p><p>The harmony moved toward the Great Hall.</p><p>Severus sped up, rounding a corner. It was me, a sixth year Gryffindor who just started at the school.</p><p>My long blond curls fell behind my back, which was turned to him as I walked toward the dining hall for dinner.</p><p>He took a deep breath before speaking. "Miss Whitlock."</p><p>The singing stopped and then I turned to see the potioneer towering over me.</p><p>My turquoise eyes looked up at Severus in surprise. I didn't say a word.</p><p>We stared at each other until Severus broke the silence.</p><p>He sighed. "Miss Whitlock, please keep your voice down."</p><p>With that, the raven haired man swept elegantly into the Great Hall, leaving me confused.</p>
