    <h2 class="title heading">
      Son Of Thor And Loki
    </h2>
    <h3 class="byline heading">
      <a rel="author" href="/users/ILoveSlash1996/pseuds/ILoveSlash1996">ILoveSlash1996</a>

        <div class="summary module" role="complementary">
          <h3 class="heading">Summary:</h3>
            <blockquote class="userstuff">
              <p>Harrison Odin Lokison-Thorson once was Harold Sirius Potter now he's the son of two Gods Loki and Thor.
