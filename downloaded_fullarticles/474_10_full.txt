<p>I'm sorry, Luna, but I'm going to need you to repeat that!  You could not have possibly said what I heard you say!</p>
<p>Luna sighed, shaking her head, Harry, I understand your shock but it's really the only way to prevent the Ministry from taking me and using me as their pet seer for the rest of my life.   I know that it's not accepted in the muggle world but this is not the world you grew up in.   But if it makes you feel better I'll repeat myself.   I need you, Harry James Potter, to claim me, Luna Selena Lovegood, as your personal slave in payment of the life debt I owe you for destroying You Know Who's body when you were a baby.</p>
<p>He shook his head in disbelief, Okay, that's what I thought you said, but, isn't there any other way? I was treated as a slave my entire life by my relatives and I refuse to treat anybody else the way I was  treated!</p>
<p>Luna smiled sadly, Harry, if there was another way, don't you think that Daddy, Hermione and I would have found it by now?  Daddy understood from the very beginning that a blatant attack on the Ministrys reputation would see him arrested and has been preparing for it ever since I suggested this over Yule.   He isn't happy about what I would have to do as your slave but I think that's just because he doesn't want to think about me having sex with anyone.   I talked to Hermione when we came back after break and the three of us have been researching for a legal way to prevent the Unspeakables from taking me.  We are all in agreement that this is the only way.</p>
<p>Wait a minute!  Hermione, the Society for the Promotion of Elvish Welfare Hermione, who is trying to free the house elves, says that you need to be a slave?     That doesn't make any sense! </p>
<p>Actually, it's the mistreatment of the elves that upsets her, she knows that you would never mistreat me, and that I'm choosing to be your slave.  I've got a note from her telling you that.  She knew that you would worry about her reaction.    </p>
<p>Reaching into her robes, she pulled out a scrap of parchment and handed it to him.
