  <b> Anything For Pie. </b>
</p>
<p>Severus is about to eat the last slice of treacle tart when his lover finds him. “Don’t even think about it, Potter. You ate the rest. This portion is mine, and I’m not sharing it.”</p>
<p>“I’d do unspeakable things to you if only-</p>
<p>“You’ll do unspeakable things to me either way.” Severus snarks, unimpressed.</p>
<p>“What if I offer to worship you from head to toes?”</p>
<p>“Not special enough. Sorry.”</p>
<p>“Rimming?”</p>
<p>“No.”</p>
<p>“Dancing?</p>
<p>“Try harder.”</p>
<p>“Opera?”</p>
<p>“Nope.”</p>
<p>“Marriage?”</p>
<p>Severus drops the plate. “D-did you just offer-?”</p>
<p>“Marriage. We haven’t done that yet.”</p>
<p>"Harry, I-</p>
<p>“Say yes, please.”</p>
<p>“Yes. I-yes, Harry. <em>Yes.</em>”</p>
