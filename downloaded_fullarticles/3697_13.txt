  <b>HARRY WAS NEVER REALLY THE TYPE TO BACK DOWN FROM A CHALLENGE.</b>
</p><p align="center">
  <b>AND DRACO WELL, HE <em>WAS</em> A CHALLENGE.</b>
</p><p align="center">
  <b>AND TO MAKE THINGS EVEN WORSE, THESE TWO SHORT-TEMPERED TEENS WERE BOTH CAPTAINS OF HOCKEY TEAMS THAT WERE NOTORIOUS FOR THEIR LONG-STANDING RIVALRY.</b>
</p><p align="center">
  <b>AFTER GROWING UP AND CONSTANTLY HEARING ABOUT ONE ANOTHER IN THEIR HOUSEHOLD(S) THROUGHOUT THEIR CHILDHOOD YEARS, THE CHANCES OF A SOMEWHAT CIVIL HOCKEY PRACTICE AND SEMI-POSSIBLE FRIENDSHIP QUICKLY GOES UP IN FLAMES DURING THE PRE-WEEK PRACTICE AT HOGWARTS ACADEMY; CAUSING DRACO TO LEAVE WITH A BLACK EYE AND BRUISED LIP, AND HARRY TO LEAVE WITH A DISLOCATED SHOULDER AND NUMEROUS SCRATCHES.</b>
</p><p align="center">
  <b>A WEEK PASSES, AND THE DAY OF THEIR CHAMPIONSHIP GAME FINALLY ARRIVES; BOTH TEAMS BLOOD-THIRSTY AS THE CROWD(S) CHEER LOUDER WITH EVERY PASSING SECOND. THE CAPTAINS STEP FOOT INTO THE CENTER, SHAKE HANDS, AND BRING OUT THEIR INFAMOUS LINES; THE AIR NOW FILLED WITH TENSION AS THEY AWAIT THEIR NEXT MOVE(S.)</b>
</p><p align="center">
  <b>AND THE MINUTE THE WHISTLE BLEW, </b>
</p><p align="center">
