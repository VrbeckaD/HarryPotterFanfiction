          <div class="userstuff"><p>Where everything is hidden-</p><p>The things this room has seen...</p><p>Students, teachers, good and evil, humans, creatures, laughing or afraid.</p><p>It is not a single room of course, and not just due to its magic- there is one in almost every universe.</p><p>And quite some are now connected- portals installed in it, for however short time, are something the room- rooms!- will remember to operate.</p><p>It is sentient in itself, that room, and quite content in its form.</p><p>The room of requirement sees and hears more than anyone would think- many have passed by it.</p><p>Where everything is hidden, indeed.</p></div>
