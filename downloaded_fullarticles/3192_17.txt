    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p>"Time is sometimes marked in seasons, and seasons are associated with the weather."</p>
<p>Over time, to be more specific, over the course of one summer the year he became of Age as determined by his Creature blood, Draco learned to control how his emotions now manipulated the heavens, and had gotten it mostly under control so that it did not happen unwillingly on his part.</p>
<p>He'd known, of course, that when he found his Mate that this control might slip, but he'd felt confident in his ability, even if his parents both just smiled and sadly shook their heads when he assured them of this.</p>
<p>As such, his reaction when he first sensed his Mate nearby took him, and the rest of those in the Great Hall that first day back completely by surprise when the monstrous rainstorm so abruptly broke out above everyone.</p>
