          <div class="userstuff"><p>
  <b>
    <img src="https://66.media.tumblr.com/c5d7155bd11ae2eb00ebd6ef7c9b6e1c/3a92e016e59291a2-61/s1280x1920/b7b8b43946cca037f109bd70ba9f85f28c7a80df.jpg" alt="" width="582" height="511" align="center"/>

<p> </p>
<p><b>Epistolary: </b> <b> <em>adjective</em><br/></b><b>Literary work in the form of letters.</b></p>
<hr/>
<p> <em>9 May 1998</em></p>
<p>
  <em> Draco, </em>
</p>
<p>
  <em> I know I am probably the last person you expected an owl from, but please read this in its entirety before you decide to toss it in the rubbish bin.  </em>
</p>
<p>
  <em> What the Wizengamot did to you was completely unfair—throwing you in Azkaban without even a trial? And in solitary confinement no less?! What an injustice! Not to mention inhumane. </em>
</p>
<p>
  <em> While I don’t condone your actions or beliefs, I do believe every witch or wizard is deserving of a fair trial.  </em>
</p>
<p>
  <em> Therefore, Draco Malfoy, I vow to make sure you get the trial you deserve.  </em>
</p>
<p>
  <em> You don’t need to write me back—I am not even sure if you’re allowed parchment and quill to respond—but I will owl you with information regarding my progress as it happens.  </em>
</p>
