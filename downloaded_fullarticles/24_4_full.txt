    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p align="center">
  <b>June 2001</b>
</p><p>When Pennywise opened the front door, he hadnt exactly expected a red-faced and bleary-eyed Henry Bowers to be standing on the other side. It had been a long time, almost half a year, since hed seen him, and that was at their usual Christmas Eve party. Taking a long look at the 28-year-old, he began to decipher the reason for his abrupt and unscheduled visit.</p><p>
  <span>The younger mans ashy blond hair was in a state of disarray and he looked like he hadnt gotten a good nights sleep in a few days.
