    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p align="center">
  <em><strong>Four Generations; Four Wars:</strong> each generation more fucked up than the last, brought together by a dying Magic.</em>
</p><hr/><p align="center">
  <strong> <em>Riddle-Era</em> </strong>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>The war against Grindelwald raged across mainland Europe. Sheltered in the safety of Hogwarts, the students realize they may not be all too protected when an attack hits far too close to home.</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <strong> <em>Marauder's-Era</em> </strong>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>The First Wizarding War grows violent. Hogwarts is a political and actual battleground, nowhere is safe.</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <strong> <em>Golden-Era</em> </strong>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>Voldemort has risen and all is not well. Umbridge wreaks havoc across Hogwarts, no one is safe.</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <strong> <em>Next-Gen</em> </strong>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>Hogwarts is gone. Destroyed. A war against humanity wages across the world. Muggles vs Wizards and the muggles are winning. Hunted down to near extinction, nothing can save them. A far more dangerous evil lurks in the shadows, waiting to strike.</em>
</p><hr/><p align="center">
  <em><strong>Magic is dying</strong>. She has gone through all possible scenarios and only one will save her - if, that is, they can put their differences aside and make it work.</em>
