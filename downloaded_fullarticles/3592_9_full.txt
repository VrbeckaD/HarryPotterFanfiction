          <div class="userstuff"><p>“Come on, Ron. I need to do something for Hermione. I feel really bad about stealing you from her,” Lavender insisted. </p>
<p>“I don’t know that she sees it that way, Lav,” Ron replied. </p>
<p>“Ron, I got pregnant while you were still LIVING IN HER FLAT!” Lavender growled. </p>
<p>Ron shook his head. He had stayed in Hermione’s flat a long time after they had ended their romantic relationship due to both a comfy sofa and he hadn’t wanted to return to The Burrow. But nothing he had said had made Lavender believe anything other than she had stolen Ron right out from under Hermione’s nose. </p>
<p>“But Lav, do you know what all those potions will do if you use them all at once?” Ron asked.</p>
<p>“No, but they can’t be harmful, can they? They all seem to do the same thing…” Lavender trailed off as she thought of something. “Um, maybe you should have Hermione see George as soon as possible.”</p>
<p>“Lav, what is it?”</p>
<p>“I don’t know if some of those potions might mess with some of the potions Hermione has been taking to remove that scar on her arm,” Lav said. </p>
<p>“Fuck!” Ron shouted as he headed for the floo. “George! George!”</p>
<p>“What is it, Ronniekins?”</p>
<p>“George, I think Lav might have poisoned Hermione. We need to get whoever helped you reverse that last mistake you made with the truth potions over to her flat right away!”<br/>George nodded, then reached into his pocket for a little green snake. </p>
<p> </p>
<p>Hermione had been sleeping in on Saturday morning after having spent a night out with her girl friends at the baby shower they had for Lavender. The pounding on her flat door woke her up. </p>
<p>“What is it?” Hermione grumpily mumbled as she opened the door. There stood George, Ron, Lavender and-- “Professor Snape?”</p>
<p>“I’m no one’s professor anymore, Miss Granger, and do have the decency to put on some clothing, would you?” the man in question snarked. </p>
