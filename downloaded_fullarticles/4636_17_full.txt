</div><div>
  <p>“Of course I remember. I don’t have any brain damage.” Harry paused. “I mean, how hard did I hit the floor?” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“You didn’t.” Ron said, a smirk on his face. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Shut up, Weasel.”</p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Malfoy didn’t let you hit the floor.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“I said, <em>shut up</em>.” Draco refused to meet Harry’s eyes now. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“He caught you. With his arms. He didn’t even use any sort of levitation charm. Hermione and I were walking out of the Great Hall to see how it went and we caught him carrying you here.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>Harry could now see Draco was definitely blushing at that, and trying to hide it by staring at the floor. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Oh, I’m never going to let that one go,” Harry said. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>Draco looked back up, smirking despite the fact his face was still red. “And you think I’m going to let it go that you <em>fainted</em> trying to ask me out?” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Which one of you told!” Harry yelled accusingly. Both of his friends refused to meet his eyes. “You traitors!” He laughed. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Bloody hell, what even made you faint? I can’t possibly be as dreary and cold as a dementor.”</p>
</div><div>
  <p>Ron’s head shot up. “Oh, you’re nothing like a dementor— Harry actually wants to kiss you.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Ron!” Harry grabbed his wand off the beside table and threw it at his best friend. It hit him lightly in the forehead. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Fuck you,” Ron mumbled lightly through his laughter. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>Draco was still blushing and now Harry was too. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“This is bullshit. I’m getting out of this. I’m fine.” Harry stood up and began to walk out of the hospital wing. He didn’t need to be down there and if Madam Pomfrey wanted to hunt him down later she could. He was leaving. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>His friends— and Draco— followed him out. They all loitered awkwardly outside the door, unsure of where to go from here. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“I think we’re going to give you two a moment,” Hermione said, taking Ron’s hand and leading him away. As they left, Harry could hear Ron complaining “...but, Hermione...” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>Harry shuffled a little awkwardly, looking at his feet until Draco cleared his throat. He looked up, meeting Draco’s eyes. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Is there still something you’d like to ask me?” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Yes, actually.” Harry took a deep breath. “Did you do the yoga thing on purpose?” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Yes.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Oh. Okay.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>There was an awkward pause. “Is that all you want to ask me?”</p>
</div><div>
  <p>“No.” Harry took another deep breath. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“You’re not going to faint again, are you?” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Not sure.” Harry tried to laugh about it, but it came out a bit weak. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>Draco leaned forward and Harry began to panic. Then, he said quietly. “I am going to say yes if that eases your nerves any.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>Harry blew out a sigh. “Yes. It does. Immensely. Thank you.” Then he met Draco’s eyes again and his breath hitched once more. It actually was somehow still scary. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Malfoy, will you...” He swallowed. “Would you like to go out with me?” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Yes, Potter. I would.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>Harry burst into a smile. Even though he knew Draco would say yes, it still felt amazing to hear. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“On one condition.” Harry froze in fear. “We stop with the whole last names thing?” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Oh definitely. I’ve been calling you Draco in my head for a while now.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“I mean, it is only fitting you don’t call your boyfriend by his last name.”</p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Yeah.” Harry froze again. “Merlin. Is that what we are now?” An excited smile broke out on his face. “Are we really boyfriends?” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“<em>Yes</em>, Potter.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“I thought we just agreed—“ </p>
</div><div>
  <p>Draco rolled his eyes. “I’m saving it for when I’m frustrated with you.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>Harry smirked. “Frustrated with your <em>boyfriend</em>.” Draco rolled his eyes again. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“I want to hear you say it again. Say you’re my boyfriend.” Harry was almost giddy now and it seemed to transfer over to Draco, who was now also smiling uncontrollably. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Harry Potter is my frustrating boyfriend.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Beautiful.” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Not like you.”</p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Ooh, that was smooth.” Harry now realized how close they were together. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Expect more of it.” Draco stepped a tad closer. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“Oh, I will.” They both leaned in, kissing in the hallway outside of the hospital wing and ignoring the cheers from down the hall that were definitely started by Ron. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>Harry pulled back first, both blushing. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>“I like this better,” Draco whispered. </p>
</div><div>
  <p>Harry slid a hand into Draco’s hair. “Why don’t I show you what I like better?” </p>
</div><div>
  <p>They kissed again, and neither were seen until morning. </p>
