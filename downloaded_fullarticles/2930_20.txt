        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p align="justify">
  <b>1976</b>
</p><p align="justify">James watches her from afar. Freckles bloomed on her skin over the summer. Her laugh sends shivers down his spine.</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her chest rolls with her breath like waves on the sand,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's clouds and mountains the rivers in our land,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's crowds and fountains she's here and she's gone,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's the sound of the sun at the crack of dawn,</em>
</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her eyes are jungles unmet and unseen,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's wild and a child, she's only sixteen.</em>
</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="left">
  <b>1977</b>
</p><p align="justify">James watches her from afar. She hates him yet he falls in love with her everyday. It's a spell she has him under. She is entrancing.</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her tears roll with her breath like the sea unchanging,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's birds and the wind and she's always leaving,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's words and she sings and the world keeps turning,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's the taste of the sky and light when it's fading,</em>
</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her eyes are jungles unmet and unseen,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's strong and she's wrong, just turned seventeen.</em>
</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center"> </p><p align="justify">
  <b>1978</b>
</p><p align="justify">James watches her snuggle into his arms. She no longer hates him. In fact, it is his lips that she kisses in the dead of night, when she knows no one is watching.</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her laugh rolls with her breath like the core of the earth,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's cold and distant she doesn't know her worth,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's bold and brilliant she's death and she's birth,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em> Her laugh rolls with her breath like the core of the earth, </em>
</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her eyes are jungles unmet and unseen,</em>
</p><p align="center"><em>She's </em> <em>grown</em> <em> and </em> <em>alone</em> <em> and almost eighteen.</em></p><p align="center"> </p><p align="left">
  <b>1979</b>
</p><p align="justify">James watches her walk down the aisle. She no longer cares about who sees them together. The world is broken but they aren't. They are James and Lily and they are meant to be.</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her hair rolls with her breath like fire on skin,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She loves and she fights in a war she can't win,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She loves and she writes she's stretched too thin,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em> Her hair rolls with her breath like forest in the wind, </em>
</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her eyes are jungles finally met and seen,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's tied she's a bride and only nineteen.</em>
</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="justify">
  <b>1980</b>
</p><p align="justify">James watches her stomach rise and fall. She's round with love and more beautiful than she has ever been. The world is still broken but they're so happy.</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her blood rolls with her breath like love in her veins,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She cries and she laughs she builds a life in her belly,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She cries and she tries and she has two hearts and a baby,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>Their lives roll with her breath like blood in their veins,</em>
</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her eyes are jungles immense and mighty,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's a wonder and a mother, she's only twenty.</em>
</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="left">
  <b>1981</b>
</p><p align="justify">James closes his eyes on the certitude that she will be safe. He falls on the ground with the certitude that she cannot die. For Lily Evans is infinite.</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her life rolls with her breath like petals on snow,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's scared and there's a thousand things she doesn't know,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She's the wind and she's lightning but she's too slow,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>Her life rolls off her chest like petals on snow,</em>
</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <em>Her eyes are clouds where only love is true,</em>
</p><p align="center">
  <em>She bled and she's dead, won't turn twenty-two.</em>
