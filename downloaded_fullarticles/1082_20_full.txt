        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p align="justify">'You know, Potter, this is getting old,' he drawls and his voice creeps up my spine, smooth and haughty.</p>
<p align="justify">I said I wouldn't come anymore, that we were done, that I was done with him, never wanting to see his face again. Yet here I am. Again. </p>
<p align="justify">I can't help it. When the days get hard, I always come to him. </p>
<p align="justify">I don't know why... Ha! A lie. </p>
<p align="justify">'Just like you,' I say because I don't have anything better. I feel ashamed I came, I have no self-esteem and I am so annoyed with the whole day I couldn't think straight and that's why I'm here. Because he's always on my mind when I'm angry and nervous.</p>
<p align="justify">He laughs. 'What a comeback, Potter.' I shrug, I don't care. 'So?' </p>
<p align="justify">It hangs between us, the question we both know the answer to. He stands in his door, not letting me in. What a prick, it's not like we've never come to blows before the last time.</p>
<p align="justify">'So?' I ask back. 'Will you let me in?'</p>
<p align="justify">He tilts his chin higher, looking down at me, smirking. 'Why would I? You made it clear the other day.'</p>
<p align="justify">If he thinks he'll make me beg...</p>
