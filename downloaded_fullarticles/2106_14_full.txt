  <em> As your rival, I find it in my best tastes to prove my superiority to you in every way imaginable to man. Hence, the writing of letters. </em>

  <em> Potter, </em>
</p><p>
  <em> You are an absolute tool, you know. Weasley is unworthy of your status. I feel it my duty to inform you of general politics, seeing as you are so gravely uninformed. </em>
</p><p>
  <strike> <em> There are two types of people - </em> </strike>
</p><p>
  <strike> <em> Think of magic like a soup. You put in the wrong ingredient, the whole meal is polluted- </em> </strike>
</p><p>
  <strike> <em> I am bloody </em> better <em> than him, why would you refuse me- </em> </strike>
</p><p>
  <strike> <em> No one’s ever refused </em> me- </strike>
</p><p> </p><p>.</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> Potter, </em>
</p><p>
  <em> I think we may have had some misgivings in our first meeting. I just wanted to- </em>
</p><p> </p><p>.</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> Potter, </em>
