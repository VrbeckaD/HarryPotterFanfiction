  <div class="userstuff module" role="article">
    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p dir="ltr">
  <em>September 1st, 1917</em>
</p><p></p>
<hr/><p></p><p><strong>Daenerys Targaryen</strong><b>was</b>nervous. Nervously <em>excited</em>, as per usual. Which was predictably how she felt <em>every</em> morning on this day for the past five years.</p><p></p><p><em>Here we go. Year number six...</em> she thought, clutching the leather handle of her suitcase tighter.</p><p></p><p><em>This will be the best year yet.</em> Dany promises herself. She exhales a long, shuddering sigh reeling out the ironic contrast of anxiety and relief, now settling down in the pit of her stomach.</p><p></p><p>To any observing outsider, she looked like any other returning student, donning the traditionally black, school robes of her Gryffindor house. However, to anyone returning to Hogwarts... well, they knew she wasnt like the rest at all. No<i></i></p><p>
  <i><br/>
</i>
</p><p></p><p>Daenerys Targaryen did not <em>blend</em>.</p><p></p><p>Everyone knew her and almost everyone <em>avoided</em> her. Especially when she was so distinctly chained to her most sinful last name. No dark magic involved, he puffs out a breath, But yeah, uhm, not bloody likely."</p><p></p><p>Greys face contorts back in thought, still confused. But how?</p><p></p><p>"Well, those were obviously <em>dementors</em>. Tyrion laughs, I mean, <em>how</em> many times have we read about them in class? Does he really think that we wouldn't have... <em>noticed</em>them? Or that nobody would talk to each other about what they saw? We all go to the same school</p><p></p><p>Well if that were true, Dany furrows her brow at him, though she did have to agree. "Then what reason would they have to possibly be on <em>our</em> train for? Arent they only supposed to be guarding the prisoners in <em>Azkaban</em>?"</p><p></p><p>She was definitely not bringing up the part about her hearing voices, right now either. More likely, if she <em>ever</em> tells anyone about that bizarre experience, she was probably slipped some Veritaserum. Naturally acquired legilimency leads to madness. Everyone knew that. She would never mention that to anyone, ever.</p><p></p><p>"Hmm, dementors out of Azkaban," he blinks back a pair of wary eyes. "Well, that's the better question, Im afraid. Either someones escaped or"</p><p></p><p>No, unsatisfied, she huffs at him, "I think there was something else. She nods suspiciously, Yes. Something dangerous came on board. But not a person. A <em>thing</em>." She crouches down to carefully crawl over towards the door.</p><p></p><p>Tyrion giggles, A <em>thing? </em>Dementors don't go after <em>beasts</em>, Dany. Only psychotic inmate murderers...</p><p></p><p>Dany slips her fingers to the edge of the door, just to slowly slide it open, just a smidge.Missandei reacts fast, reaching for her arm. No stop" she inhales sharply. Dany shrugs her off.</p><p></p><p>"<em>Dany</em>? Dumbledore said to keep the cabin doors <em>closed</em>. We're not allowed to open them."</p><p></p><p>Ignoring her completely, she was already stealthily peeking out into the hall."No, actually he <em>advised </em>us, so its not... She squints, trailing off, tryingto catch a glimpse down the empty aisle. Then she stops, in rigid recognition.</p><p></p><p>
  <em>Wait- What if the monsters still on the train?</em>
</p><p></p><p>
  <em>Or worse, what if its</em>
</p><p></p><p>
  <em>That boy.</em>
</p><p></p><p>Her lips curve downwards subtly, as the blood oily drains from herface, slowly processing the sight before her. Quietly holding her breath, her eyes fixate on the collapsed boy with soft dark hair, laying limp on the ground.</p><p></p><p>
  <em>What </em>
</p><p></p><p>She narrows her eyes, softly confused.</p><p></p><p>
  <em>That cant be a monster... hes so...</em>
</p><p></p><p>
  <em>Hes... just a boy just an innocent-looking boy?</em>
</p><p></p><p><em>     But who is </em>he</p><p></p><p><em>Or who,</em>was<em>he...</em>she thinks guiltily. Was.</p><p></p><p>Was he dead or just knocked out? If thats the same boy, and she <em>knew</em> it was his thoughts sounded like he was suffering the dementors true kiss.</p><p></p><p>And it felt like it too.</p><p></p><p>Wow, she really was insane.</p><p></p><p>Grim, aiding professors hover over the boy in an attentive circle around him, blocking the rest of her vision.</p><p></p><p>She turns to Tyrion solemnly, Its... its a boy. Here. Can you tell who it is? ...I think, that hes dead. She slides back, making room for him to see for himself.</p><p></p><p>Unsure of the situation, he peers over at a nodding Greyworm, and then in contrast, at a frowning Missandei. And though still skeptical himself, he leans his face forward anyway, peeking out through the slightly open crack.</p><p></p><p>She sighs a few moments later, impatiently waiting, as he still observes the scene.She thinks for a moment, remembering something her uncle Illyrio had told her before she left. About an expelled transfer student.</p><p></p><p>Oh</p><p></p><p>Of course? Thats it! It has to be him!</p><p></p><p>
  <em>The one everyones been talking about. The one everyones afraid of.</em>
</p><p></p><p>Well? Who is it? Do you know him?" She pesters.</p><p></p><p><em>Though wouldnt he have been on board with them when they left? And worse, she couldnt even imagine what</em>else could have broken in onto a <em>moving train,</em> then?</p><p></p><p>"One second, Daenerys, Tyrion chides.</p><p></p><p>Then suddenly, he sucks in a breath, gaping, "Oh wait a - <em>Merlin</em>, that cant be? <em>Hmm</em><em></em>"He pulls back in short realization, quietly closing the door with him.</p><p></p><p>"Well, he was definitely attacked by a dementor, they were feeding him chocolate. He distracts her comedically, his dark tone lightening, But he <em>was</em> sitting up, so I dont think that he's dead, Dany. Though he did look dreadful..."</p><p></p><p>She purses her lips sharply."I'm serious, Tyrion. Who is it? She presses, <em>I know that you know</em>. I can tell when you know something.</p><p></p><p>Oh, so clever, you are. His eyes first dart over suspiciously towards the door; before he glances back at her, nodding. Okay, he takes a breath, leaning in,"Okay... so I'm almost certain, that that's<em>Jon Snow</em>." He whispers grimly, "The transfer boy, the one thats been in the <em>Prophet</em>." Danys eyes light up curiously.</p><p></p><p><em>It is him</em>.</p><p></p><p>Jon...<em>Snow</em>?" she prompts, innocently enough, just recordfor more information.</p><p></p><p>Missandei scowls at them. "Wait you mean,<em>The</em>Jon Snow?" she questionsrigidly, now shaking her head. "...My father told me all about him He told me to stay away from him. He said he's dangerous.</p><p></p><p>No, I dont think so. Dany bites her lip.</p><p></p><p>Missandei eyes her cynically. "You dont think so? You dont even <em>know</em> him? What do you mean... you dont,<em>think</em> so?</p><p></p><p>I have heard of him actually. Now that I think about it... she hums, And he doesnt seem like a murderer.</p><p></p><p>Uhm, Dany, no havent you seen the papers? They say his parents were both<em>muggles</em> And that he was <em>expelled</em> from <em>Durmstrang,</em>for classified reasons. All coincidentally <em>right</em> <em>after</em> all of those muggleborn students were killed."</p><p>
  <img src="https://yelmermotmans.files.wordpress.com/2016/02/giphy-61.gif?w=487&amp;h=298" alt="" data-pagespeed-url-hash="239320650" onload="pagespeed.CriticalImages.checkImageForCriticality(this);"/>
</p><p></p><p>"Why move the boy to <em>new</em> school if he so dangerous?" Greyworm asks flatly.</p><p></p><p>"<em>Exactly...</em>I don't know. Why <em>would</em> they send him here?" She produces a false smile, her dimpled, warm almond cheeks, not quite reaching her strict eyes, "I mean, to actually allow him to <em>finish</em> his academic career, at <em>Hogwarts</em>it's absolutely mad."</p><p></p><p>
  <em>She could be so ignorant sometimes.</em>
</p><p></p><p>"Wow... And what does him being <em>muggleborn</em> have to do with him getting expelled?"</p><p></p><p>Is <em>that</em> all you got from what I said? Missandei scans her thoughtfully. "Thats not what I meant, and you know that. I was just repeating, from what I saw in the papers and what my father told me. He just thinks that the boy must have seriously endangered the lives of those students to have gotten expelled like that. Possibly even had a hand in it"</p><p></p><p>No, I dont believe it. Dany sniffs, No one has enough information. Its all speculation.</p><p></p><p>Well, theres a lot of speculation from a lot of reliable sources Dany, if you bothered to check the facts. And you know whats evenworse" her voice lowers darkly, "Some people say he really did<em>kill</em>his parents..."</p><p></p><p>Dany snorts sadly. "He was a child<em>. </em>How could you say that?"</p><p></p><p>Tyrion rolls his eyes, then points out, You know, you actually seem to know <em>a</em> <em>lot</em> about this Jon<em>Snow</em>, Dany?</p><p></p><p>She didnt actually, that was the problem.</p><p></p><p>"I'm <em>not</em> saying that. Missandei defends, ignoring Tyrion, All Im saying, is that its not a crime to be cautious. I mean, dont you know who the last wizard to be expelled from Durmstrang was?"</p><p></p><p>The properly postured Ravenclaw exhales a small frustrated sigh, Okay, well... directing it at their cluelessly innocent shrugs. Heres a hint, it was <em>sixteen</em>years ago..."</p><p></p><p>Tyrion bows his head in realization then whispers faintly, "<em>Gellert Grindelwald."</em></p><p></p><p>Dany crosses her arms."Well, those are two very unrelated things she argues flatly.</p><p></p><p>Its all in the The Prophet, Dany. The two seem to be too similar to be a coincidence, Missandei raises her brows pointedly.</p><p></p><p>Exactly. Dany twists her mouth in thought, staring severely out the window. This is why I stopped believing in<em>the papers</em> a long time ago, ever since they sent my father to Azkaban."</p><p></p><p>Tyrion chokes out a laugh, "Oh,<em>did you really now? </em>He scrunches his nose sarcastically,<em> Only since then?</em>"</p><p></p><p>Greyworm smirks at him. Dany just glares back at him with a shrug.</p><p></p><p>"So you mean, you've<em>never</em>believed in them, Tyrion chuckles. Considering, you couldn't even <em>read</em>yet, when that happened... he gestures, you know, when you were a newborn baby."</p><p></p><p>She turns determined, eyeing him coldly, "Im not joking, Tyrion. I want to find out the truth. And the truth, is not in those tabloids.</p><p></p><p>He groans languidly, pinching the bridge of his nose."Ohhh no. Nope,<em>no</em>.</p><p></p><p>She scowls back at him, <em>What</em>...</p><p></p><p>It is <em>too</em> early for this Dany, please. I mean, we haven't even gotten to school yet?" He shoves a handful of jellybeans into his mouth, "You know I like to get settled in he cringes, puckering at the flavor, before you, you know, <em>drag</em> <em>us</em>alonginto one of your... you know, <em>causes</em>."</p><p></p><p>A storm of ideas had already begun flooding her mind minutes ago, propelled exponentially by her need to be right. She glares out the window, pondering the possibilities.</p><p></p><p>Tyrionexamines her telling expression. Squinting at her slowly. Really, what now?</p><p></p><p>"Nothing?" She beams quietly. I dont know what you mean.</p><p></p><p>His face falls flat. "Daenerys. His expression drops coolly, Let me just tell you what I know, for certain he warns, If there is<em>any</em>kind of correlation... between this... Jon Snow, <em>and</em> those soul-sucking dementors... which, there definitely <em>is</em>... he looks her square in the eyes.</p><p></p><p>Then the truth, is something, I don't think you want to know."</p><p></p><p></p>
