        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p>Neville was used to Luna joining him in the greenhouse, so he didn’t realize immediately that she was working on something.</p>
<p>Usually, while Neville did his repotting, she would find an open spot on one of his worktables to sit, with a pile of editing for <i>The Quibbler</i> or a book on creatures that might not be real.  But today, she had gathered a skirtful of flowers, weaving the stems together with her delicate fingers.</p>
<p>“There,” she said, and Neville only caught a glimpse of the wreath before she placed it on his head.  “Perfect.”</p>
<p>He laughed.  “Yes, it is.”</p>
