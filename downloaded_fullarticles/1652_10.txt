        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p>She was floating-</p><p>Space and time, nothing mattered anymore.</p><p>She had fought, bravely, and at the time it had seemed like the right side, the right choices to make.</p><p>Friends standing by her side.</p><p>It had took her too long to notice that most of them were turning away...</p><p>"Extremist" she had been called, "lost" even by her best friend.</p><p>It was time to leave.</p><p>Cayla was dead, her purpose gone.</p><p>When had she started to define herself over fighting him? </p><p>She doesn't know. She barely cares.</p><p>Lucandra doesn't want to exist anymore, but a part of her...</p><p>Lucy doesn't want to die. </p><p>Irrational fear.</p><p>She has seen the first step of death, knows she will have the choice to stay there or go on.</p><p>It won't be a real death, not yet, not ever if she doesn't want to.</p><p>And yet...</p><p>She won't die. Would it be suicide, even after thousands of years? </p><p>She can't greet death as an old friend.</p><p>But she can greet time.</p><p>And her memories are left in space...</p><p>She was floating. </p></div>
