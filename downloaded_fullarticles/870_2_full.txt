        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p><strong>First</strong> <strong>year</strong></p>
<p>Jasmine Lillian Potter was sitting in her seat next to her best friend Hermione Granger and listening professor McGonagall's explanation on how to turn their pets into goblets. Just as she had started writing notes, she felt someone tugging her hair.</p>
<p>"Malfoy?" Jasmine asked, shocked to see the blonde slytherin behind her.</p>
<p>For a second she thought she saw his cheeks reddening but his scowl was back on before she could make sure of what she had seen. Her eyes narrowed in suspicion and she pursed her lips together like she had seen her aunt do countless times before.</p>
<p>"What do you want?" Jasmine asked quietly, fearing that McGonagall would hear her.</p>
<p>Malfoy smirked and leaned closer to Jasmine. She blushed and quickly leaned back but Malfoy just snorted. Sighing, she leaned back so Malfoy could say whatever he wanted to say.</p>
<p>"I jus wanted to say..." Malfoy whispered in her ear, "that your hair is... quite ugly."</p>
<p>Just as the bell rang, Jasmine got up and slapped Malfoy, maybe a bit too hard but she really didn't care at the moment.</p>
<p>"You're so annoying!" She yelled, stomping her foot and marching out of the classroom.</p>
<p>Jasmine could swear she saw regret on Malfoy's face but when she asked Hermione about that, she told her that she was being absurd.</p>
<p>
  <em>{§§§} • {§§§} • {§§§}</em>
</p>
<p><strong>Second</strong> <strong>year</strong></p>
<p>Jasmine was sitting at the lake, doing homework with Hermione and Ginny when she saw Malfoy walking towards them with Zabini and Parkinson. She nudged both girls who immediately became wary.</p>
<p>"Hey Potter, Beaver, Weaslette", Malfoy said, smirking when Jasmine hissed angrily.</p>
<p>"What do you want, Malfoy?" Jasmine asked, getting up from the ground with her hands on her hips.</p>
<p>She saw Malfoy smirking but before she could do or say anything, he had levitated her over the lake. Fear took over Jasmine's body and she froze, tears falling down her cheeks.</p>
