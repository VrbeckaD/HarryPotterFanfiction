  <span>~ Double Helping of People ~</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>People are always asking me, "Greyback, why are you always eating people?" My question for them is why should I want to eat anything else? I am a monster, after all. Eating people is just one of the things that monsters do. If there was a handbook for </span>
  <em>
    <span>How to Be a Monster in Ten Easy Steps</span>
  </em>
  <span>, step two would read as follows: Eat people.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>People are delicious. Other meats no longer satisfy my hunger. The deathcry of an animal does not appeal to my senses in the same way as the screams of a human. And the begging. I have an ungodly love for listening to someone beg me to spare their life.</span>
</p>
