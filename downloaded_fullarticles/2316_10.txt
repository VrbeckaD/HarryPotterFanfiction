    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p>He had done it. All three spiritual stones and the Ocarina. He had gained access to the mythical realm. A sword placed at the entrance drew his eye. Deep blue hilt and gleaming silver blade, driven deep into the marble pedestal that creeping vines were beginning to take over.</p><p> </p><p>
  <span>He didn't think. He grabbed the hilt in a two handed grip and pulled, finding the strength to extract the stubborn blade. Mere seconds after he tugged the blade free, he crumpled into a heap of green clothing, his breathing even and quiet.</span>
  <span> </span>
</p><p> </p><p>"Thank you, Hero," a voice said, chuckling darkly as he passed the limp body of the green-clad boy.</p><p> </p><p>The dark figure paused, then turned to look at the boy who had pulled the sword. A decision was made that day. A decision that would change the fates of two worlds forever.</p><p> </p><p>The evil man pulled a knife from his belt and crept up to the peacefully sleeping boy...</p>
