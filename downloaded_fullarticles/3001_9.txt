    <p>STORY TITLE:</p><p><strong><b>GRIMM MAGIC</b></strong> by <strong><b>MYSTICWRITER3018</b></strong> (AO3) </p><p> </p><p>STORY SUMMARY:</p><p>AU, HP-Thirdyear, Alternate-Universe/Parallel-Different Worlds, Grimm/other Fantasy movies and TV--creatures, OC</p><p>Harry had enough of Dumbledore and his manipulative ways when he was denied of his godfather Sirius Black and living with him. He ran away when the opportunity came and arose for him, found Sirius and Remus, and they all ran away through a magic portal to get away from the corrupted universe they’re in, along with their friends Draco, Luna, and Neville for the trip.</p><p>They stumble into a whole new world, a different alternate parallel universe, where magic is the same from their previous world--but there are very certain differences in it with unfamiliar unique creatures known as the <em>Wesen </em>and the people that were known to look after, guard and watch over, and hunt the bad-type Wesen, are called the <strong><b>Grimm</b></strong>.</p><p>Harry Potter--renamed as Harry / Hadrian Evans-Black--didn’t think that he, his pseudo-family and friends would still have their magic in the other universe, nor were they revered among the Grimm and the Wesen, as a mystical clan of Grimm that can wield and use magic in the other world.</p><p>Harry also didn’t think and knew that in this other world, there would be markings upon them that would signify who they are fated to, like soulmates. And he has two intricate kinds of markings--that specifically means that he has two soulmates. One, another Grimm…and the other, a Wesen.</p><p>Follow this amazing story about Harry and his family and friends living their lives in the other new world…a world where they start over and have a happy life, and were given second chances to gain what they want or wish for. </p><p>Follow the story…that is GRIMM MAGIC. </p><p> </p><p>OOOOO</p><p>
