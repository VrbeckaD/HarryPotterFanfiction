<p align="center"> </p>
<p>“I don’t know, I swear I don’t know.” The words tumbled from Hermione’s lips, over and over again. The pain was never ending and a tiny part of her just wanted it to stop. She liked to think she was a strong person, but in this moment she would have told Bellatrix anything to just make it stop.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>“Insolent mudblood!” Bellatrix hissed and stood, shaking finely as she looked down at the girl.</p>
<p> </p>
