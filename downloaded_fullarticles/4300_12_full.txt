          <div class="userstuff"><p align="center">
  <strong> <em>You are cordially invited to an All Hallows Day Ball on November the 1<sup>st</sup> at Hardwick Hall. </em> </strong>
</p>
<p align="center">
  <strong> <em>The hosts Sirius Orion Black and Remus John Lupin require all guests to hand in their wands at the beginning of the evening, they will all be returned at the end of the event. </em> </strong>
</p>
<p align="center">
  <strong> <em>Please respond via owl as soon as possible.</em> </strong>
</p>
<p>
  <strong> <em>November 1<sup>st</sup></em> </strong>
</p>
<p>
  <strong>9.30pm</strong>
</p>
<p>
  <strong> <em>Hermione’s POV</em> </strong>
</p>
<p>Hermione stood in the conservatory of Hardwick Hall enjoying the peace and quiet. She had only attended the event because her secret boyfriend had asked her to. She hated parties and hated the majority of people at this one even more so. Suddenly she felt something heavy hit her head causing her to fall to the ground. The last things she heard before she passed out were an object falling to the floor and footsteps running away.</p>
<p>
  <strong>10.30pm</strong>
</p>

<p>“Thank you for coming,” he watched Sirius greet his secret girlfriend as she arrived at the event. Remus was the only one who knew the two of them were dating, not because he was informed but because of his Werewolf senses, the pair practically reeked of each other. Whilst part of him wished Sirius would tell him officially, he knew the reasons the pair were keeping a secret.</p>

Out of the corner of his eye, he spotted a couple sneaking out of the Greenhouses. Both men were dark-haired and one was sporting a trademark scar on his forehead. The men seemed extremely close with how they were walking hand in hand and seemed to lean into each other.</p>

Hermione hated this habit and he really meant to quit but it was stressful being an Auror and the stupid bint wouldn’t date him so what right did she have to nag him over his <em>disgusting </em>habits as she called them. Finishing the cigarette, he dropped it to the ground to stub it out when a figure caught his eye.</p>
<p>“My, my Snape,” he grinned to himself, “What on earth are you doing here?”</p>

<p>“Not all of us can look effortlessly perfect like you,” she flirted.</p>
