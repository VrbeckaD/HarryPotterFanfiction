        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p>I am not waiting for anything.</p><p>Every tick of the clocks becomes a deafening chant of death for my ears, by now accustomed to that evil and unholy sound.</p><p>I do not want anything.</p><p>I feel my thoughts wrapping around me, aware that darkness is inside of me, is <em>part </em>of me.</p><p>No sun is able to stain with its light my existence.</p><p>The only thing I am waiting for, the only one I demand, is for your face to come back and lighten up this darkness, to give me back all those years spent being nothing more than a mask, than a shadow.</p><p>Because I am a shadow, I live in a shadow.</p><p>In <em>your </em>shadow, Lily.</p></div>
