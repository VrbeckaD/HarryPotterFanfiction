          <div class="userstuff"><p>Harry lifted his head out of the pensive with one alarming revelation: he had to die.</p><p>He gazed at the basin of the pensive, where his own reflection gazed back, out of focus. It was a wonder he survived all these years, he supposed, what chance did he have anyway?</p><p>The throb on his scalp was a welcome reprieve. Harry continued tugging his hair, hoping it was all a dream — but he knew it wasn’t.</p><p>‘Harry, where are you going?’ asked Hermione, rightly so, after Voldemort’s ultimatum.</p><p>‘I know what you’re thinking, mate. Don’t do it.’ whispered Ron, frowning.</p><p>‘Just one last thing I want to do first.’ explained Harry, who walked towards the corridor he last saw a certain blonde, pointy, pureblood. Ron and Hermione followed him, but he thought, fuck it all.</p><p>Malfoy looked surprised to see Harry head his way; he turned away, focusing his eyes on the floor, but Harry was having none of that today.</p><p>‘Malfoy.’</p><p>‘…’</p><p>‘I’m going to kiss you.’</p><p>‘Potter—‘ began Malfoy, who was promptly interrupted with a deep kiss ‘—<em>mphhh</em>.’</p><p>‘Always wanted to do that.’ finished Harry with a smirk. ‘See you.’</p><p>Malfoy’s mouth was left hanging.</p><p>Now, to the forest.</p><p><b><em>End</em></b>.</p></div>
