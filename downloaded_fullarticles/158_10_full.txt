    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <h1 class="western" align="center">Chapter 1</h1><p class="western" align="CENTER">
  <em>"Even Angels would be homesick in this forsaken town."</em>
</p><p class="western" align="CENTER">
  <span>
    <em>The Ataris  Unopened Letter to the World</em>
  </span>
</p><p class="western" align="CENTER"></p><p class="western"></p><p class="western">
  <a name="OLE_LINK10" rel="nofollow" id="OLE_LINK10"></a>
  <a name="OLE_LINK11" rel="nofollow" id="OLE_LINK11"></a>
  <span>Draco leaned casually against the wrought iron railing that protected the dim-witted masses from tumbling in their inebriated state from the second floor to a most unwelcome stop below. He smoked a cigarette, sucking the smoke in his lungs and holding it there before releasing it in an expanding cloud. In his other hand he held a tumbler of scotch on the rocks. His keen, piercing gaze surveyed the dancing crowd below him, thronging to the hypnotic beat, rubbing their bodies together like a distorted orgy where, in this case, everyone was clothed. He loved it. Watching these people thrash and flail in ecstasy on </span>
  <span>
    <em>his</em>
  </span>
  <span> dance floor warmed by a bluish glow emanating from underneath.</span>
</p><p class="western"></p><p class="western">
  <span>Everything was tinged with that same faint blue glow: the lights in the corners offering pale illumination to those sitting close at private tables, the hovering strobes that painted the people dancing below. Thick glass walls filled with bubbles and distortion and layered with privacy charms, partitioned the two-story wizarding club, offering some seclusion to the patrons while still allowing them to be a part of the constant party.</span>
</p><p class="western"></p><p class="western">
  <span>He took a sip of his amber drink, snuffed the cigarette out in an ashtray, and walked towards the open stairs heading down. He didn't walk as much as strut, swaying side to side like a gangster king patrolling his royal hood, chin high like a regal head of state, and to him the masses did bow.</span>
