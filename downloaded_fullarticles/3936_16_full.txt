I'm sure we'll have many things to discuss."</p><p> </p><p>Bella's eyes sparkled with excitement. "Yes, Sir!"</p><p> </p><p>As he left, the Headmaster regarded her with a frown. "Do you know that man, Miss Black?"</p><p> </p><p>She shrugged. "I've heard of him, Headmaster. If you don't mind me asking, why was he here?"</p><p> </p><p>"He was applying for a teaching position."</p><p> </p><p>"Oh?" The idea of being taught by her Lord thrilled her like nothing else.</p><p> </p><p>Dumbledore narrowed his eyes at her. "Alas, I had to tell him the position had already been filled."</p><p> </p><p>
  <b>Liar.</b>
</p><p> </p><p>"Oh." She sighed. "What a pity. He's a great wizard."</p><p> </p><p>"In my experience there are two kinds of great wizards, Miss Black. Those that should be admired and those that should be feared. I would be careful with the admiration in this case."</p><p> </p><p>He nodded and walked off.</p><p> </p><p>Bella smiled to herself. "Perhaps... but how can I admire someone who doesn't instill fear in people?"</p><p> </p><p align="CENTER">***</p><p><a name="Bella_ran_along_giggling" rel="nofollow" id="Bella_ran_along_giggling"></a>Bella ran along giggling with excitement, her new husband in tow. He wasn't reluctant per se, but he always seemed to be a few steps behind.</p><p> </p><p>"Come on, Rodolphus!" she said breathlessly. "We're almost there. Can't you smell the greatness?"</p><p> </p><p>He merely grunted and followed her as she entered the secret meeting place.</p><p> </p><p>Finally. After biding her time for years and following his diary's every direction, she'd received an invite.</p><p> </p><p>Her Lord had his back turned when she entered, but as soon as she stepped into the room he turned around. A new thrill coursed through her, as his eyes landed on her.</p><p> </p><p>
  <b>Hand over the diary.</b>
</p><p> </p><p>She nodded. She knew she'd lose the voice, he'd told her as much, but she'd gain so much more. Wordlessly she walked over to her hero, her saviour, the love of her life, and handed him the book.</p><p> </p><p>He received it with a smile.</p><p> </p><p>"Welcome, Miss Black. I've been waiting for you."</p><p> </p><p>"It's Lestrange now." She glanced at her husband.</p><p> </p><p>"Ah, yes. Of course. I'm delighted to have the both of you here."</p><p> </p><p>She grinned back. This was her future, her fate. He would lead her and she would follow.</p><p> </p><p>Always together.</p><p> </p><p>She couldn't wait.</p><p> </p></div>
