        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p>It's 1997, Harry Potter's fifth year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Our story begins in the room of requirement where Parvati Patil is desperately trying to produce a patronus charm after a DA meeting, with Anthony Goldstein unknowingly watching her.</p><p>Parvati shouted, "Expecto patronum!"</p><p>A tiny wisp of silver issued from the end of her wand.</p><p>Parvati muttered, "I give up."</p><p>Anthony said, "Don't give up, you were doing so well."</p><p>Parvati jumped slightly and asked, "Were you watching me?"</p><p>Anthony nodded and stated, "I think you should keep at it."</p><p>Parvati pointed out, "But I still haven't managed to do it yet."</p><p>Anthony replied, "I struggle to produce a patronus charm too."</p><p>Parvati mumbled, "It's too hard to think of just one happy memory."</p><p>Anthony corrected her, "You're supposed to think of your happiest memory."</p><p>Parvati responded, "Yes I know that, but I find it hard to pick out my happiest memory."</p><p>Anthony smirked. "Maybe this can be your happiest memory."</p><p>He pulled Parvati into an unexpected yet passionate kiss.</p><p>Parvati smiled. "Let me try it again."</p><p>Anthony grinned. "I'm waiting."</p><p>Parvati screamed, "Expecto patronum!"</p><p>A silver elephant erupted from the end of her wand and walked around her.</p><p>Anthony beamed, "So my kiss worked?"</p><p>Parvati answered, "Yes; Tony, your kiss worked."</p><p>Anthony said, "I'm glad that I'm in your happiest memory, Parv." </p><p>Parvati smiled. "I'm glad you're in my happiest memory too."</p><p>She pulled Anthony into a long, lingering and passionate kiss which left him speechless.</p></div>
