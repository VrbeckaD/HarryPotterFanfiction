        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p>Our story begins in the corridor where the Fat Lady hangs, little does she know that a lonely Hufflepuff is about to come across her.</p><p>Ernie Macmillan was trying to find his way to Defence Against the Dark Arts, but he was very, very lost. </p><p>He approached the Fat Lady, but when he came face to face with her; he couldn't utter a word.</p><p>The Fat Lady boomed, "Yes, Hufflepuff; what do you want?"</p><p>Ernie came out of his trance at her words and muttered, "I'm lost, Ma'am."</p><p>The Fat Lady asked, "Where are you meant to be?"</p><p>Ernie told her, "Defence Against the Dark Arts, but I think it's fate that I ended up here."</p><p>His face turned scarlet.</p><p>The Fat Lady smiled. "Ah, so you believe in fate; young Hufflepuff?"</p><p>Ernie nodded.</p><p>The Fat Lady beamed, "I do too."</p><p>Ernie replied, "I think we were meant to meet right here, right now, like this. I'm Ernie, by the way. Ernie Macmillan."</p><p>The Fat Lady answered, "The Fat Lady." She added, "But you can call me Bernie."</p></div>
