  </em>

  <span>It was a Friday when they gathered they’re house into the common room. Furniture had been moved to create a wide open area. With that, the dancing lesson began. Muggle music twisted out from a seventh year’s wand while a sixth year changed their clothing. With smiles that seemed to be more at home on Fred and George’s face, they pulled their housemates to the makeshift dance floor. Hours later, as they all fell asleep (the moon near its peak) the children from the magical world dreamed of dancing.</span>
