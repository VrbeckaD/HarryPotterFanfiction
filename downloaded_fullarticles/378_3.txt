          <div class="userstuff"><p>Neville had just finished re-potting the hellebore, packing their roots with fresh morning snow, when he heard raucous laughter.</p><p>Rounding the corner of the greenhouse, he smiled as he saw Harry's boys as well as a few of their classmates having a snowball fight.</p><p><i>Gryffindor versus Slytherin, just like old times,</i> he thought to himself. </p><p>That Al and Malfoy's son were friends was a bit of a surprise but a welcome one.</p><p>As he watched, he couldn't help but notice that the Gryffindors were a bit older—James's class were fourth years—while the Slytherins were also outnumbered by two.</p><p>Well, that wouldn't do. </p><p>Neville slowly bent and packed a snowball, waiting and hoping his target would move just a little bit closer.... </p><p>"Where'd that come from?" someone shouted as James took a direct hit in the back and everyone looked around for the perpetrator.</p><p>Neville tried to look innocent, though when Al spotted him, he winked, and the Slytherins cheered. </p><p>"Oof!" he exclaimed as he felt what must have been a snowball to <i>his</i> back.</p><p>Spinning around, Neville joined in the laughter as he and the students watched the headmistress brush off her gloved hands and retreat back inside the castle.</p></div>
