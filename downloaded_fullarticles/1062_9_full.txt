    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p>In one world, James Potter is wandless when he faces Voldemort.</p><p>In another, hes not.</p><p>...</p><p>Lily, take Harry and go! Its him! Go! Run! Ill hold him off-</p><p>The door blasts inwards and Lily runs. She hears, distantly, over the heartbeat loud in her ears- roaring, hissing, crashes. James has always been best at transfiguration.
