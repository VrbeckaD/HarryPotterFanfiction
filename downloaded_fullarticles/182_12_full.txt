    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p dir="ltr">        This work is a collection of various x Reader drabbles, headcanons, and preferences, cross-posted from Tumblr. Tags will be updated as I go. Feel free to make a request! Below is the prompt list I've used for almost all of the drabbles here. <a href="https://nicolejones412.tumblr.com/post/145312706640/prompts" rel="nofollow">Here is the source for the list</a>, from @nicolejoones412 over on Tumblr.</p>
<p> </p>
<p dir="ltr">1. “I can’t leave her there all alone, surrounded by stone walls.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">2. “It doesn’t matter. You weren’t using those memories anyway.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">3. “You’re right above ‘clown’ on my creepy scale.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">4. “Cookies? You’re bribing me with cookies? I mean, they’re good, but not that good.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">5. “Please remain calm and stay indoors.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">6. “What does your shadow do while your sleeping?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">7. “We can’t have a crisis - my schedule is already full.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">8. “Once we cross the bridge, nothing will be the same. You’re still ok with that, right?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">9. “Get away from him/her!”</p>
<p dir="ltr">10. “We’re bribing you with the antidote. The poison will kill you/him/her in three days. Think about it.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">11. “I don’t go looking for trouble but I do enjoy befriending it.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">12. “It’s official. This is the worst case I’ve ever worked on, and that’s including the one with a dog as a suspect.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">13. “On a scale of one to Australia, how dangerous are we talking?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">14. “What do you mean work with him/her? He/She tried to kill me!”</p>
<p dir="ltr">15. “I’m trying my best to be polite, but if you move that knife a centimeter closer to me I will rip you apart.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">16. “Please stop putting things in my microwave.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">17. “No, I said we were safer, not safe.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">18. “You here to finish me off, sweetheart?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">19. “We’ll make a criminal out of you yet.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">20. “I think I broke him.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">21. “Don’t bleed on my floor.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">22. “And it doesn’t bother you that you almost killed him/her?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">23. “Is this one of those times when you want me to lie to protect your delicate emotions?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">24. “The best way to keep a prisoner from escaping is to make sure he never knows he’s in prison.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">25. “On your left.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">26. “A villain is just a victim whose story hasn’t been told.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">27. “I see humans but no humanity.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">28. “Look at you, sacrificing yourself for others! When did you get a heart? I thought you lost your moral code?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">29.“How do you destroy a monster without becoming one?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">30. “Shut up that was one time.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">31. “Today I did not have to kill anybody. Today was a good day.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">32. “Is it like a requirement for super villains to go to lame pun school?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">33. “I thought they’d killed you. I lost my temper.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">34. “Technically speaking they used to be people. Bone-chilling isn’t it?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">35. “It’s your choice.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">36. “I know how it ends. Of course I know. I just forgot how it began.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">37. “Never underestimate my ability to be idle.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">38. “I couldn’t have been more wrong.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">39. “Now where’s the fun in that?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">40. “I didn’t know that idiocy caused people to start spontaneously bleeding from the nose.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">41. “The last time I said yes to you, a lot of people died.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">42. “Hearts are like bread dough. The more they’re played with the harder they become.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">43. “For God’s sake, who have you killed now?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">44. “I can’t lose you!”</p>
<p dir="ltr">45. “I am not human. I never was. So why do expect me to act like one?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">46. “We had a deal.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">47. “You’re like a blister. You only show up once the hard work is done.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">48. “What does the little blinking light mean?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">49. “Don’t move. They’re watching you.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">50. “Why is he/she bleeding?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">51. “Sorry I died.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">52. “Well, good luck with that.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">53. “Did you honestly think that I would leave you there?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">54. “What’s wrong?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">55. “How can you expect me to trust you after what you did?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">56. “You’re a what now?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">57. “It’s not fleeing. It’s advancing in the other direction.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">58. “Here’s some new about my life up until now. It’s boring.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">59. “I’m not supposed to laugh, right?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">60. “Oh, look, a bunny. You know the last known bunny attack was, well, never.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">61. “That’s a shine caused by love.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">62. “A guy does something stupid once, that’s because he’s a guy. If he does the same stupid thing twice he’s usually trying to impress some girl.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">63. “Girls, girls, you’re both pretty. Can I go home now?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">64. “Boys, if you can’t play nice, you can’t play together.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">65. “If only there was someone out there who loved you.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">66. “I’ve loved you since the first moment I saw you. And I’ll love you until my last.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">67. “Please don’t go! Don’t leave me here!”</p>
<p dir="ltr">68. “Sometimes it’s good to be scared because it means you still have something to lose…”</p>
<p dir="ltr">69. “Why didn’t you try harder? You just left.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">70. “So you hate me now too? Join the club.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">71.  “I’m never good enough, no matter what I say, no matter what I do.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">72.  “You took a piece of me, and I let you.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">73.  “I’m not alright! Okay? Are you satisfied? I’m not alright.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">74. “I’m not going to say goodbye to you again, do you understand me?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">75. “Is that what you’re doing? Trying to make me hate you?”</p>
<p dir="ltr">76.  “Go ahead, laugh at the girl who cares too much.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">77. “You’re leaving because it’s easier to walk away than to fight for what you really want.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">78. “Pretending not to love you was the hardest thing I ever had to do.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">79.  “Even though I know you’re lying, I still almost believe you.”</p>
<p dir="ltr">80.  “How long have you known?”</p>
