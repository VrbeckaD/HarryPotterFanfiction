<p>“Harry? What...” but Ginny was interrupted when Luna swung into the room on a rope. Ginny had no idea where the rope came from, much less what it was attached to.</p>

<p>“Wench?! Walking the... Oh, right,” deduced Ginny, finally. “I'll just go wish Hermione a happy birthday then, shall I?”</p>
