        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p>Today was the day! Harry had been hoping and planning for this ever since he and Draco had started dating. It was their one year anniversary.They had became boyfriends exactly a year ago, and Merlin, was Harry glad they had. Draco was his everything. Today was the day Harry was going to ask.</p><p>They both had work today, so Harry wasn't planning anything too crazy. He had planned dinner and was sure to get off of work with enough time to prepare it. On Sunday, he had borrowed some candles from Molly, hoping to give their small kitchen a romantic feel.
