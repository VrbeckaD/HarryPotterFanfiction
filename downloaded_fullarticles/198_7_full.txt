      <a href="/works/12095070/chapters/27418491">Chapter 1</a>
    </h3>

  </div>

  <div class="userstuff module" role="article">
    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p>
  <span>Julian walked into his office he shared with Barry. He set down his coffee cup and took off his jacket and hung it up. He sat down and took a sip of coffee when he heard footsteps approaching. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“You are always late Allen, even with that speed of yours, why is that?” Julian said never looking up</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I’m actually early” Hermione answered</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Julian looked up and saw Hermione standing in front of him. Her hair was incredibly short, it resembled a pixie cut, he almost didn’t recognize her. Standing up, he looked behind her to see if anyone else was there. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“What are you doing here Granger?” Julian asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Looking for you Malfoy”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Do not call me that”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I see you’re living as this Julian Albert Desmond, what kind of name is that? You were hard to track down” Hermione said looking down at the name plate on his desk</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Now that you’ve found me, leave”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I need to speak to you about something”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“No, you have to leave”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Before your coworker comes in and finds me here?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Exactly, leave now”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Then you’re coming with me”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I know you haven’t been here long, but here in America you just can’t just up and leave your work place whenever you feel like”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I’m sure you can find an excuse, car trouble, cat’s sick, you need more coffee”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione pushed his coffee cup off the table and it splattered all over the floor.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Granger! Stop this!” </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“A quick scourify will fix it”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Stop that! These are muggles, no maj, have you forgotten!?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I just need to speak to you for a few minutes and then you’ll never have to speak to me again”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“If only it were that easy”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Barry came walking in holding coffee and a box of half eaten donuts. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Julian, and friend. Really hot looking friend. I mean you don’t have any friends, I mean you don’t have friends that come visit you here at work” Barry said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“It doesn’t matter, she was just leaving” Julian said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Donut?” Barry offered</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Oh yes, at least someone here has manners” Hermione said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione took one of the donuts and smiled at Barry.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I’m Hermione, thank you for the donut” Hermione said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I’m Barry, how do you know Julian?” Barry asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione took a bit of the donut and looked over at Julian who looked very uncomfortable.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“We went to school together” Hermione answered</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“A long time ago” Julian added</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Not that long ago, would you believe that he used to dye his hair an unnatural shade of blonde? That it was almost white?” Hermione said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Why?” Barry asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“It was a phase I grew out of. Anyways, you should get going” Julian said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Oh yes, Julian has decided to take me out for breakfast since I’m only here a short while” Hermione said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“You’re already eating breakfast” Julian said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“It’s ok, I’ll cover for you, that’s what we do, you know on team fla..I mean cause we’re friends”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione looked back at Draco who was standing there confused. This Barry was more like Neville from what she had seen, and Draco would never be friends with someone like that. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Did you hit your head?” Hermione asked Julian</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“No” Julian responded</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Are you sure?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Let’s go, it’s rude to keep a lady waiting” Julian said grabbing his jacket</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Barry watched the two of them leave. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Friend huh?” Barry said</span>
</p><hr/>
<p>Hermione was walking in the nearby park with Draco who looked annoyed. She finished up her donut and walked with him.</p>
<p>
  <span>“What do you want Granger?” Draco asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Why are you living as a muggle here?” Hermione asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Why do you care?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Because it was hard to find you. And this is the last place I’d expect to find you, living with muggles. What would your pureblood parents say?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I wouldn’t know, they’re dead”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Oh, I’m sorry, I didn’t know”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“It was all over the papers, my father sent to Azkaban, my mother died of overwhelming grief when my father received the dementer’s kiss.”</span>

<p>After lunch, the two ended up at a candy shop. Most of the younger people didn’t realize who they were and ignored them. Draco had a sweet tooth and ended up buying far too much candy. Draco was eating a licorice wand, when Hermione pulled it from his mouth and took a bite from it.</p>
<p>
  <span>“There are plenty more in the bag, I don’t know why you had to take mine” Draco said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Because I wanted to. And you were never going to share”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Well now you’ll never know”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“So are you going to invite me over or not?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I didn’t want to be presumptuous”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Always a gentleman. Must be that pureblood upbringing. Since you’re not going to invite me over, I’ll invite you over.”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“And where are you staying? Potter? Weasley?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Neither, I’m in a hotel right now. It’s rather cramped at Harry’s, and Ron and I aren’t exactly speaking”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Weasley not talking to you? Now that’s a story I need to hear”</span>
</p><hr/>
<p>Julian returned to Central City taking the plane rather than port key. He didn’t want anyone to know where he was traveling to. When he landed he checked his messages and they were all from a frantic Cisco. He took a cab over to Star Labs where he found everyone in a panic.</p>
<p>
  <span>“Julian, you’re back!” Cisco said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“What’s happened?” Julian asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“So we know who Savitar is” </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“That’s good”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“It’s Barry”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“What?!”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Not real Barry, fake time remnant Barry”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“What?!”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“And Caitlin hs full on Killer Frost now”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“When did that happen?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“You missed a lot while you were gone”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Miss me boys?” Killer Frost said as she appeared, “You should really change the security codes”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Julian and Cisco ducked under the tables when Killer frost shot ice at them. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I did not come back to this” Julian said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I called Barry, but he seems to be tied up” Cisco said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Julian reached into his bag to find his wand. He held it in his hand and felt the magic thrumming through his body. He stood up, holding his hand at his side.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“What are you doing?!” Cisco yelled</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Finally stopped hiding?” Killer Frost teased</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I’ve had a very tiring trip back” Julian said as he cast a shield in front of him</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Poor baby, let me make it all better” </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Julian!” Cisco yelled</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Killer Frost sent ice at him, but it bounced off the invisible shield Julian had cast. Killer Frost was surprised but then sent a blast of ice at him again. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I’m rather tired right now. How about we move things along?” Julian said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Julian pointed his wand at her and then cast an unforgivable, Imperio. Killer Frost stood there with a blank expression on her face.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Be a dear and walk yourself into one of the cells and lock yourself in there” Julian said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Killer Frost began walking towards the cells. Cisco stood up and looked at him in amazement. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Where can I get one of these?” Cisco asked</span>
</p><hr/>
<p>“So you’re not a meta” Cisco said</p>
<p>
  <span>“No, we covered that already” Julian said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“But you’re not human”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Not exactly”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Then what are you?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I’m a wizard. Magic is real. All of it is real”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Whoa. No way. Like pointy hats and dancing brooms and Mickey Mouse causing trouble?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Brooms don’t dance”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“So witches are real”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Yes”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Your hot friend, she’s a witch!”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Yes”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Why did you hide it for so long then? Do you know what we could have done if we had known?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I’m not allowed to tell any muggles, no majs about magic. You humans are suppose to think that there’s no magic in the world. We keep our worlds separate”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Why? Why would you do that? I mean magic is so cool!”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Rules, I don’t make them. You have to swear never to tell anyone, otherwise they’ll come and oblivate you”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“That doesn’t sound fun”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“No, they take your memories away, they’ll make you forget”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Amnesia! Does that mean people who have amnesia are just people that knew about magic?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I don’t know! It could be a possibility. Or it could be the fact that they have actual amnesia”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“But they could be because of magic”</span>
</p><hr/>
<p>Julian watched as the rest of the team did as Savitar held Iris hostage. He watched as Barry tried to fight him but failed. Savitar was going to kill Iris. Julian going against everything he had tried to achieve in the past few years. He raised his wand and cast the same spell that was used on him.</p>
<p>
  <span>“Sectumsempra!” Julian yelled</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Savitar started screaming and dropped Iris to the ground. Barry flashed over and grabbed her and ran off with her. There was the sound of metal being scraped and torn open. Savitar clawed out of his metal suit, screaming and bloodied. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Did you do that?” Cisco asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Yes” Julian answered</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Can you make it stop?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“No”</span>
</p><hr/>
<p>Draco waved his wand in the air, packing what few belongings he had into a bag. He stopped and then emptied his bag. There wasn’t anything that he wanted. This life he had created for himself wasn’t one he recognized. He opened his window and stuck his head out.</p>
<p>
  <span>“Bubo bubo!” Draco yelled</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>A eagle owl flew down from a tree and landed on his windowsill. He reached over and stroked its feathers. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I’m sorry I neglected you. Will you forgive me?” </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>The proud owl merely looked at him but preened under his touch. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I need you to deliver something for me”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>The owl hooted. It had been so long since it had delivered anything. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I just need to go get it, I’ll be back in a few minutes”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>The owl looked at him aghast. Making him wait? </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Julian walked out of his flat and headed over to the local bakery to pick up some donuts. He knew Barry had an affinity for them and smiled. He bought a small box and then returned home. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Can you handle this Bubo?” Draco asked as he tied the box to his leg</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>The owl merely hooted as if he were insulted. He was an owl, a wizard’s owl, not a carrier pigeon.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Off you go” Draco said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>The owl took off and flew out his window. Julian left his things and his apartment. He took his phone with him and walked out. He walked past the coffee shop where Iris, Cisco, Wally and Joe were talking. He walked past the bus stop where Caitlin was waiting for the bus. He walked past Star Labs and towards the water. He looked around at where he had spent the past year and sighed. Barry was gone. He walked into the speed force to take savitar’s place. His friends wouldn’t remember him, because the aurors here were very fast at responding when they realized he was using magic, from an unregistered wand, and casting a spell that could be counted as an unforgivable. The no maj or muggles were oblivated to forget his very existence. Julian Albert merely wrote papers on meta humans. He never worked with Caitlin’s mother to formulate a cure for her. And he never joined team Flash. With a crack, he apparated away.</span>
</p><hr/>
<p>Hermione was talking to Harry in his office when the eagle owl started tapping on the window. Harry opened the window to let the bird in. The owl landed gracefully on the desk, and looked at Hermione. When Harry tried to untie the box, the owl bit him.</p>
<p>
  <span>“I don’t think it’s for me” Harry said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>Hermione untied the box and the owl hooted. She opened the box and smiled at what was inside. She saw a postcard tucked under the paper and picked it up. It was a picture of a city skyline. She turned to the back and it was blank. </span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“That’s strange” Hermione said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>There was a slightly glow and words appeared on the back. She smiled. She brought the postcard to her lips and kissed it, leaving her lipstick print on it. She looked around Harry’s desk for an envelope and placed it inside. She then tied it to the owl, who took off out the window. Hermione took a bite out of the donut inside.</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Who’s sending you donuts?” Harry asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“If I give you one will you drop this whole thing about Ron?” Hermione asked</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“For the next 15 minutes” Harry said as he took a bite from a donut</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Just let it go Harry. We’re the same, we’re not compatible”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I didn’t say anything. I’m trying to eat a donut here”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Everyone expected me to marry him and squeeze out kids. I mean I didn’t know if I wanted to spend forever with him!”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Still eating, not listening”</span>
</p><hr/>
<p>Draco walked out onto the observation deck at the empire state building. It was windy and filled with tourists. He’d stop using the self tanner on his skin and it turned back into its pale porcelain tone. He stopped dying his hair and it returned to his natural platinum shade. He walked around looking at the city skyline. He felt someone standing next to him and looked over.</p>
<p>
  <span>“Granger, decided to finally show” Draco said</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“You didn’t give the greatest directions Malfoy”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I was in a bit of a rush when I left”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“What happened?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I tried to save someone, used magic in front of the muggles. A lot of magic, the aurors were there within seconds. Oblivated them”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I’m sorry Draco”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“They’re better off without me”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“You can’t say that”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I just did, besides disaster averted, no one got killed, and everyone is safe. Guess that’s all that matters right?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“I suppose. The hero gets the girl. The sidekick gets praise, and they all live happily ever after”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Does the hero get the girl?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Sometimes the girl gets the guy she wants”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Even if he’s a deatheater?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Former deatheater”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Let’s get out of here”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Your place or mine?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Does matter now does it?”</span>
</p>
<p>
  <span>“Not really, let’s go”</span>
</p>
