          <div class="userstuff"><p>Shaking hands.<br/>
My fingers don’t know<br/>
how to touch you,<br/>
inexperienced.</p>
<p>My feelings are as<br/>
raw as the look on<br/>
your face when I<br/>
begin to bend.</p>
<p>Going to my knees,<br/>
a supplicant seeking<br/>
relief, daring the dark<br/>
fire in your eyes.</p>
<p>Your words can either<br/>
flay me open and leave<br/>
me bleeding on your<br/>
floor or finish me entirely.</p>
<p>When you let me<br/>
strip you bare,<br/>
take you in,<br/>
it’s indescribable.</p>
<p>Is there anything like<br/>
patient scorn to teach<br/>
one to savor a bit of<br/>
danger, a slight bite?</p>
<p>Elegant hands.<br/>
Yours shaking now<br/>
as you touch me;<br/>
you’re experienced.</p>
<p>It doesn’t make a<br/>
difference because<br/>
now it’s us, finally<br/>
alone in the dark.</p>
<p>A candle wavering<br/>
splashes light over<br/>
our writhing skin,<br/>
bodies entwined.</p>
<p>I’ll learn from your<br/>
iron restraint, and<br/>
you’ll warm to my<br/>
ebullient desire.</p></div>
