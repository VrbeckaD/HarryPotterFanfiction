<p align="center">
  <em>"You are not wrong, who deem</em>
</p>
<p align="center">
  <em>That my days have been a dream;</em>
</p>
<p align="center">
  <em>Yet if hope has flown away</em>
</p>
<p align="center">
  <em>In a night, or in a day,</em>
</p>
<p align="center">
  <em>In a vision, or in none,</em>
</p>
<p align="center">
  <em>Is it therefore the less gone?</em>
</p>
<p align="center">
  <em>All that we see or seem</em>
</p>
<p align="center">
  <em>Is but a dream within a dream."</em>
</p>
<p align="center"> </p>
<p align="center">
  <strong>- Edgar Allen Poe, ‘A Dream Within A Dream'</strong>
</p>
