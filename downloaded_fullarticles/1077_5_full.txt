    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p></p><p>It was Wednesday, which meant that Harry was en route to Ron and Hermiones. The kids would be done school soon, and Harry dubbed this part of his day the care and feeding of magical creatures. Rose and Hugo were enough of a handful themselves, but on Wednesday, he also had Teddy, and, on the odd day that Mrs. Weasley was busy, Victoire and Dominique. It was bedlam, pure and simple, and Harry was never happier than when he had a houseful. He pulled the minivan into the garden, and stepped outside. Hermione had done something impressive with the wards, so he didnt have to worry about trying to Alohamora the door. Cleverest witch of our age, he thought to himself happily, as he entered the cluttered and homey cottage and began preparing snacks.</p><p></p><p>The floo whooshed green, and a pile of children erupted from the hearth, their excited chattering interrupting the peace and quiet. Teddy, laughing, said Uncle Harry! We had a Niffler at school today!</p><p></p><p>A niffler! Harry exclaimed in mock surprise. Was he naughty?</p><p></p><p>Ever so! Rose crowed. Its not sponsible, having such a bad creature near children</p><p></p><p>Did your mum teach you that word? Responsible? Harry was serving milk and biscuits and the kids crowded around the kitchen island.</p><p></p><p>Sort of Rose allowed. She was telling dad that it wasnt sponsible for Uncle George to take me up on his broom</p><p></p><p>Ah Harry answered. But did you enjoy being on the broom?</p><p></p><p>Rose nodded, wiping her mouth with a napkin. </p><p></p><p>Well, my little Rosebud, its important to remember that responsibility is good. And having fun and trying something daring is also good sometimes too</p><p></p><p>Im daring! Victoire offered, her blonde hair falling into her eyes.</p><p></p><p>Indeed you are, petit Harry told her. Do you think youll be a Gryffindor when you go to Hogwarts?</p><p></p><p>Like you! she said.</p><p></p><p>Uncle Harry Dominique was the littlest, but also the most serious of these four children. </p><p></p><p>What is it, my love? he asked, ignoring the shouts of the other children to listen to her. She didnt volunteer much, so he always made sure to take the time to make sure she felt heard.</p><p></p><p>You went to Hogwarts, mais non?</p><p></p><p>I did</p><p></p><p>So why dont you have a wand?</p><p></p><p>Harry smiled a little. I have one, cheri, but I dont use it anymore.</p><p></p><p>You can do wandless magic then?</p><p></p><p>Not quite. I got hurt when I was younger, and my magic went to sleep</p><p></p><p>We must wake your magic up again! Victoire smiled brilliantly at him, the solution obvious. </p><p></p><p>Didnt your maman tell you not to wake a sleeping dragon? Harry laughed at her serious little face. It simply wasnt up for discussion.</p><p></p><p>Draco cast a quick tempus and realized that he was late. Merlin, Astoria will kill me if I miss dinner. At nearly two years old, the baby simply adored Harry, and was usually found toddling after him whenever they were together.</p><p></p><p>None of that Draco sniffed. Scorpius is going to be a Malfoy, with everything that comes along with it</p><p></p><p>Prat Harry said affectionately. Scorp, your daddy is a prat The babys eyes widened and he chuckled.</p><p></p><p>Ill be going. I need to speak to my lawyer, draw up some papers to revoke your rights as Godfather Draco teased, placing a kiss on the babys head and giving Harrys shoulder a friendly squeeze. Ill be by to pick him up in a few hours</p><p></p><p>Dont rush, well be going to get Lion tattoos after dinner Harry said.</p><p></p><p>Dinner with his wife had been lovely, Draco reflected, as he lingered over a post-meal glass of wine. Since Scorpius had been born, theyd been taking time together as they could snatch it, and Draco was truly grateful that Harry loved spending time with Scorpius as much as the child loved his Uncle Harry. As he drained his glass, Draco smoothed his robes, and flooed through to Grimmauld Place. As he stepped through the floo, the first thing he heard was crying. He smiled a little, Harry obviously had his hands full with cranky children. It served him right for stuffing them full of sugar and keeping them up too late, he thought to himself.</p><p></p><p>Whats this? he called out. The crying appeared to be coming from upstairs. Curiously, it hadnt abated, and it seemed that both Teddy and Scorpius were wailing. Harry? he called, but there was no answer. Making his way into Teddys room, Draco stopped short. Teddy was curled into a ball, rocking back and forth sobbing. </p>
