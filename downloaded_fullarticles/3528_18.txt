She and Jacob fought a lot when she was younger but banded together just as often when it came to dealing with ‘outsiders’ to their family. Their father was their primary caretaker growing up as he sat on the Wizengamot while their mother worked as an Ambassador to the French Wizarding government.</p><p> </p><p>More Headcannons: </p>
<ul>
<li>Before Hogwarts, she very much preferred to be left alone with her animals</li>
<li>Very sarcastic, to the point she was often in trouble with her parents</li>
<li>The Dark Arts interest her very much, not so much practicing as the theory behind it</li>
<li>Dreams of becoming a curse breaker or a magical theorist</li>
<li>She’s not particularly fond of magical creatures but likes the class well enough</li>
<li>Her favorite drink is root beer but butterbeer is an okay substitute</li>
<li>She loves sandwiches. Give her all the sandwiches. Now.</li>
<li>She met her mother’s second cousin, Lucius, and his family a couple of times and she worries about her baby cousin Draco being surrounded by such Purist rhetoric </li>
<li>She accidentally stabbed Jacob in the leg with his wand when they were fighting shortly before his 3rd year</li>
<li>She met Newt Scamander at an event she attended with her mother in between 3rd and 4th year and mentioned Barnaby’s interest in magical creatures. They still write to one another and Elise promised to keep an eye out for his grandson, Rolf, who was a couple of years younger</li>
