<p>Worth the Wait</p>
<p>~</p>
<p>“Impatient?” whispers Harry. “It’s my understanding this sort of thing’s standard.” </p>
<p>“Good things are worth waiting for,” replies Neville.</p>
<p>“Good answer.” Smiling, Harry says, “And congratulations, you’ve snagged one of the best girls in the world. She’s worth waiting for.”</p>
<p>“Thanks.” Neville hums. “I never asked you, but you’re okay with this…right? With us?” </p>
<p>Harry blinks, then grins. “Are you mad? I think you two’re brilliant.” </p>
<p>“Okay. Good.” The music starts, and they turn, facing the door where Ginny’s standing in her wedding gown. She’s gorgeous, radiant, and Neville exhales. He’ll never understand how Harry could give her up. </p>
