        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p>

<p>Kettle whistles<br/> Cup fills<br/> Beans scattered on the table</p>
<p>Bubble rise<br/> Tub fills<br/> Clothes scatter on the floor</p>
<p>Papers rattle<br/> Cup empties<br/> A quiet morning begins</p>
<p>Body settles<br/> Mind empties<br/> A quiet morning begins</p>
<p>Russet hair ruffles<br/> Body settles<br/> Bertie Botts hit the door</p>
<p>Chestnut curls fall<br/> Bubbles rise<br/> Water hits the floor</p>
<p>“Mione, love<br/> You can’t hide in there.<br/> It won’t go away.”</p>
<p>“Ronald, dear<br/> I am not hiding.<br/> Go away.”</p>
<p>Water cools<br/> Cup empties<br/> Beans hit the door</p>
<p>Water cools<br/> Bubbles disappear<br/> Towel falls to the floor</p>
<p>Azure eyes twinkle<br/> Restless finger twine<br/> Questions fill his mind</p>
<p>Chocolate eyes close<br/> Restless mind turns<br/> Question wanting answers</p>
<p>“Mione, love,<br/> Just get out of the bath.<br/> Bubbles won’t make it go away.”</p>
<p>“Ronald, dear.<br/> Just go get the book.<br/> Bertie Bots won’t make it disappear.”</p>
<p>Kettle whistles<br/> Cup fills<br/> Book settles on the floor</p>
<p>Drain opens<br/> Water disappears<br/> Feet settle on the floor</p>
<p>Scarred hands steady<br/> Pages turn slowly<br/> Waiting for the door to open</p>
<p>Scared eyes scan<br/> Reflections emerge slowly<br/> Waiting for secrets to reveal</p>
<p>“Mione, love.<br/> I am going to continue drinking my tea<br/> And pretend you did not say that.”</p>
<p>“Ronald, dear.<br/> I am going to continue explaining my point<br/> And pretend you are listening.”</p>
<p>Head lifts<br/> Arms open<br/> Rising on unsteady legs</p>
<p>Door opens<br/> Tears fall<br/> Walking into open arms</p>
<p>Azure eyes close<br/> Savoring her trust<br/> Holding his greatest treasure</p>
<p>Chocolate eyes settle<br/> Soaking in his strength<br/> Holding her greatest fear</p>
<p>“Mione, love.<br/> This is what I knew we could do.<br/> Trust me.”</p>
<p>“Ronald, dear<br/> When did you grow so strong?<br/> Love you.”</p></div>
