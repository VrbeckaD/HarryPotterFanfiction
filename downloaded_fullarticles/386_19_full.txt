    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p align="center">
  <em>Present day, June. </em>
</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="center">
  <strong><em>The most dangerous lies are the lies you tell yourself</em> </strong>
</p><p> </p><p>Ron’s chest rose and fell in an even rhythm next to Draco in bed. Clothes were scattered all over the soft, carpeted floor, leaving a telling trail of what they had done. Ron’s Auror’s robes were tossed in one corner, his dirty dragon skin boots in another. Then followed trousers, socks, belts, boxers, shirts… before it all ended by the feet of the bed. They were both stark naked; Draco’s body ached from having Ron’s hands on him earlier, gripping and groping every inch he could find. The hour was late and the stillness in his dim bedroom should have been soothing. It wasn’t. Draco couldn’t relax, couldn’t give in to the tiredness currently residing in his body. He turned to his right and watched Ron’s peaceful, sleeping face, eyes raking over freckles, lines and wrinkles in the pale skin. He paid attention to every little detail, noticing how Ron had more freckles on his nose than anywhere else. They seemed to gather on the tip, fighting for space, then going across the nose in both directions, continuing down his cheeks. The blush from the sex they’d had was still visible there even though it had been thirty minutes, at least, since it ended. Then were the lines. The wrinkles. Some were from laughter, some from time.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p> </p><p>They had a year left until they turned thirty, yet Ron seemed to have aged a lot the last three years. He looked older than twenty-nine. Draco noticed the dark, purplish circles under his eyes.</p><p><span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span>Sleep deprivation.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p>
  <em>Stress.</em>
</p><p> </p><p>Draco wanted to reach out and touch. Ron’s red lips were still slightly swollen from kissing, the bottom lip chapped. He had a tiny cut in the right corner of his mouth. His eyelids were lilac. His red hair was tousled, fringe stuck to one coppery brow. Something twitched inside Draco when he looked at him.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p>Which was ridiculous.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p>He refrained from touching.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p> </p><p>
  <em>He’s not really yours.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></em>
</p><p> </p><p>Weasley may come here on the regular, but fucking Granger was the one he’d married. Draco absolutely despised her. He had always despised that woman, but now he loathed her more than ever. He loathed her all the way down to the wedding band on Ron’s left ring finger. Draco always tried to avoid looking at it and pretend Weasley’s wife and children didn’t exist. In his bedroom with the expensive dark interior or in the rest of his large flat, they didn’t.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p>It was supposed to have been just sex. Draco knew what he was getting into when they got involved with each other.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p>For a while, it was okay too. It was good. Then Draco realised he was in hell. Especially when he saw Ron smile. When he saw him laugh. When he felt him. When he took his breath away. When they breathed the same air just an inch from each other…<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p> </p><p>
  <em>Stop it. He’s not really yours.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></em>
</p><p> </p><p>As he watched Ron sigh in his sleep and he moved a little, Draco decided he had enough. He turned in the bed so his back was facing Ron. It took a few seconds, which seemed like a lifetime, until Ron’s arm unexpectedly curled around Draco’s waist and Draco felt the warmness of Ron’s body against his own. It comforted him more than he would have liked to admit. Ron snuggled up close and buried his face in the nape of Draco’s neck. The coppery stubble on his chin was itching him.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p>“I know you stare at me,” Ron whispered tiredly. “Go the fuck to sleep.”<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p>“Shouldn’t you be going?” Draco hated how his voice sounded at the question. It sounded… <em>whiny. </em>But it was getting close to midnight. The clock on the wall opposite the bed quietly ticked closer and closer to a new day. Ron never stayed.   <em>So what is this then? What am I to you?<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></em>

  <em>You said you loved me. Twice now.</em>

Not now.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p>Ron said things sometimes, then acted like nothing had happened.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p> </p><p>
  <em>I want you to leave her and be with me.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></em>
</p><p>
  <em>Cause Salazar knows, I do love you.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></em>
</p><p>
  <em>I really, really, really fucking love you.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></em>
</p><p>
  <em>You selfish bastard.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></em>
</p><p> </p><p>The words always seemed to get caught in his throat whenever he wanted to express them. He couldn’t say the three words back. He brushed away the wild, flaming red hair from Ron’s brow instead with gentle fingers.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p>Draco desperately wanted to freeze time so Ron could never leave.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p> </p><p>
