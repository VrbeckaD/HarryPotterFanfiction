        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p>They were never meant to be.</p><p>They were friends from the second the met on the train.</p><p>He was a Slytherin.</p><p>She was a Ravenclaw.</p><p>It was a forbidden friendship, but they didn't care.</p><p>You would never see them apart.</p><p>He was her protector, daring anyone to try and get near her.</p><p>She was his confident, calming him down in his anger.</p><p>He started to develop feelings toward her in fourth year.</p><p>She started to develop feelings for him in seventh year.</p><p>When they went into healer training together, he acted on his strong feelings.</p><p>And she returned them.</p><p>They fell in love.</p><p>He proposed.</p><p>She said yes.</p><p>Her family shunned her, not speaking to her for years.</p><p>His family welcomed her with open arms, having changed since the war.</p><p>He stood at the end of the isle waiting happily.</p><p>She was being walked by her cousin James towards him.</p><p>They found out they were expecting two months later.</p><p>In the end, they had 11 children, 20 grandchildren, and 47 great grandchildren.</p><p>In the end, they were Mr. Scorpius Malfoy and Mrs. Rose Malfoy.</p><p>In the end, no one questioned if they were meant to be, because they knew.</p></div>
