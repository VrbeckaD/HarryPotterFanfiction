    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p>It was Harry's last month with the Dursley's and, to be honest, he didn't really mind as much. He knew he'd be saying goodbye to them soon and so he was constantly filled with excitement. Nothing they said or did truly brought him down. </p>
<p>When he got back from Diagon Alley with Hagrid, they were all wound up and annoyed with him. Uncle Vernon was red faced with anger and yelling at him, telling him to throw away his stupid bird or he'd cook it, of all things. She squealed angrily at him. </p>
<p>Aunt Petunia obviously knew that this would be the situation as she didn't look surprised by the owl at all. She just rolled her eyes and scoffed. It wasn't until Harry impulsively decided to bring out his wand and threaten to use a spell on uncle Vernon that her eyes went wide. </p>

<p>Harry told Ron about what it was like growing up in the muggle world, not knowing about magic.</p>
<p>I didnt even know I was a wizard until recently. Harry said seriously. Ron looked shocked. My aunt Petunia wasnt happy when she knew Id found out. Then Dumbledore and Hagrid has to come and explain so much to me. He laughed, leaving out <i>certain</i> parts. It was mental. </p>
<p>It sounds like it. Ron giggled. </p>
<p>I didnt even know about my parents or Voldemort. Harry shrugged.</p>
<p>Suddenly, Ron gasped. You said You-Know-Whos name! </p>
<p>So? Harry raised an eyebrow.</p>
<p>I just thought... out of all the people, you wouldnt want to, because of, well... He stammered. </p>
<p>Its just a name, Ron. It cant hurt me. And Im not scared of this man. He said seriously.</p>
<p>Okay, good. I wouldnt want you to be. Ron smiled. Does the scar hurt? He asked nervously.</p>
<p>No, its just ugly. Harry shrugged. Like the rest. He mumbled, pointing to his face.</p>
<p>Thats not true. I mean, I dont know what happened but its not ugly. Ron said suportively . You look like youve been in battle like a soldier! He grinned.</p>
<p>Harry laughed. Thanks, I suppose. But its not that cool. He sighed. I was just attacked by a stupid dog. He bluffed again. If he had to go with something, hed always go with that. He likes Ron, they could be good friends. But he doesnt want to scare him away by telling him what he is.</p>
<p>Ron just smiled sympathetically at him. Harry appreciated it.</p>
<p>Eventually conversation switched up to classes. </p>
<p>What classes do you think youll be good at? Ron asked.</p>
<p>Well, I read up on a bunch of books before coming here. I think Ill be good at potions, who knows. Harry shrugged. </p>
<p>Ron raised his eyebrows. Really?</p>
<p>Yeah. He said with some confidence. Im good at cooking considering I did it all the time for my relatives, and potion making seems as if its a little bit like cooking. He said with a grin. So, I might not suck. </p>
<p>Ron just snorted at him.</p>
<p>Soon enough, when they were way out of London and saw some sheep and cows in fields, a lady with a trolley came by. Anything from the trolley, dears? She asked with a smile.</p>
<p>Ron just held up a squashed sandwich and shook his head. Im all set, thanks. He looked and sounded miserable.</p>
<p>Harry took pity on him, hes been there. He got out the money he had in his pocket and grinned at the lady. Well take as much as we can pay for. She looked shocked for a second before laughing. </p>
<p>She handed him over a whole bunch of sweets of different kinds, sitting them on the chair next to him. He thanked her and she left. He then told Ron to dig in.</p>
<p>Wait, you did that for me? He asked with raised eyebrows.</p>
<p>Of course I did. Youre my friend. He said with a smile, then halted. Were they? Was he being too forward? I mean, thats if-</p>
<p>Friends! Ron stuck his hand out with a smile and Harry let out a relieved breath, shaking his hand. His first human friend. Not counting the serpent one back in Surrey. </p>
<p>They both laughed as they got the giggles from eating too much sugar. </p>
<p>Harry picked up something he saw someone in the Leaky Cauldron eating when he was there with Hagrid, a weird looking frog.</p>
<p>Ron, what is this? He asked confused.</p>
<p>Those are chocolate frogs. He grinned. Harry grimaced and put it down, scooting far away from it. Ron laughed. Theyre not real frogs, Harry. Its just chocolate thats charmed to move like that.</p>
<p>Harry nodded, acting as if that was whats bothering him when actually it was the chocolate itself. <i>The smell is revolting.</i> He holds in a gag. He notices the box has a card on the front though and quickly goes to snatch it up. </p>
<p>On the front of the card is a picture of Albus Dumbledore. The man who came to see him at his home. Hes smiling at him in the picture.</p>
<p>He flips the card over and on the back it reads,</p>

<p></p><center>
  <p>
    <b>ALBUS DUMBLEDORE</b>
  </p>
  <p>
    <b>CURRENTLY HEADMASTER OF HOGWARTS</b>
  </p>
  <p>Considered by many the greatest wizard of modern times, Dumbledore is particularly famous for his defeat of the dark wizard Grindelwald in 1945, for the discovery of the twelve uses of dragon's blood, and his work on alchemy with his partner, Nicolas Flamel. Professor Dumbledore enjoys chamber music and tenpin bowling</p>
</center>Harry snorts and turns the card back over. He frowns, noticing that hes gone from the picture.<p>Where did he go? He asks.</p>
<p>Hes gone. You cant expect him to stick around all day, can you? Ron asked whilst munching on some pasties.</p>
<p>They took turns trying out some Bertie Botts Every Flavour Beans, sometimes smiling at a flavour and other times gagging.</p>
<p>I swear, George reckon he ended up with a bogey flavoured one, once. Ron grimaced. </p>
<p>The train took them into darkness, where there were forests and rivers. It was a spooky but beautiful sight.</p>
<p>Soon enough, there was a knock at their door. The boy that Harry saw earlier talking to his gran was standing there, tearful. </p>
<p>Have either of you seen a toad, at all? When they both shook their heads he wailed. Ive lost him! He keeps getting away from me.</p>
<p>Hell show up. Harry said, trying to sound supportive.</p>
<p>The boy nodded and walked off. </p>
<p>I dont know why hes so upset over a toad, Id try and lose it as quickly as possible. Ron shrugged. He then glanced at Scabbers and placed him back on his lap gently. But sometimes I wish I could get rid of this thing, too. He cringed. I tried turning him yellow yesterday but nothing happened, look. He sighed. </p>
<p>He got out his wand and was about to cast a spell when the door opened again, a bushy haired girl entering. Have either of you seen a toad? A boy named Nevilles lost one. She asked in a bossy sort of voice.</p>
<p>We already told him no. Ron said and went back to his spell. He was interrupted by the girl, though.</p>
<p>Are you doing magic? Lets see, then. She grinned and sat down. Ron looked shocked. </p>
<p>He turned back to Scabbers and raised his wand. </p>
<p>Sunshine, daisies, butter mellow, turn this stupid, fat rat yellow. He waved his wand but nothing happened.</p>
<p>He sighed in disappointment.</p>
<p>Are you sure that's a real spell?" She said with a smirk. "Well, it's not very good, is it? I've tried a few simple spells just for practice and it's all worked for me. Nobody in my family's magic at all, it was ever such a surprise when I got my letter, but I was ever so pleased, of course, I mean, it's the very best school of witchcraft there is, I've heard. She rambled on. I've learned all our course books by heart, of course, I just hope it will be enough. I'm Hermione Granger, by the way, who are you?"</p>
<p>She spoke so quickly it was hard to keep up.</p>
<p>Im Ron Weasley. Ron mumbled.</p>
<p>Harry Potter. Harry nodded.</p>
<p>Are you really?" She asked with wide eyes. I know all about you, of course. I got a few extra books, for background reading, and you're in Modern Magical History and The Rise and Fall of the Dark Arts and Great Wizarding Events of the Twentieth Century.</p>
<p>"Am I?" Harry asked with wide eyes.</p>
<p>"Goodness, didn't you know? I'd have found out everything I could if it was me. She said. Do either of you know what house you'll be in? I've been asking around, and I hope I'm in Gryffindor, it sounds by far the best. I hear Dumbledore himself was in it, but I suppose Ravenclaw wouldn't be too bad... she said thoughtfully. Anyway, we'd better go and look for Neville's toad. You two had better change, you know, I expect we'll be there soon.</p>
<p>She walked off, leaving both boys gaping after her. </p>
<p>I do not want to end up in a house with her. Ron exclaimed loudly.</p>
<p>Hmm. Harry said absentmindedly. He didnt want to make too many judgements, too quickly. </p>
<p>Did you hear about Gringotts? Ron spoke up with wide eyes.</p>
<p>What? </p>
<p>Someone tried to rob a high security vault. It was all over the Daily Prophet. Ron said shocked.</p>
<p>Harrys eyes went wide. What happened to them?</p>
<p>They havent been caught! Thats why its such big news. Ron said fidgeting. They say it must have been a really powerful dark wizard to pull something like this off, if youre able to break into Gringotts. People freak out thinking its You-Know-Who. </p>
<p>Harry sighed and decided to change topic of conversation. He didnt like talking about Voldermort, he was a madman who doesnt deserve his time or energy.</p>
<p>They started talking about quidditch, Ron going into deeper details of explaining the rules than Hagrid did and telling him about all the different teams. Harry actually thought it sounded interesting and decided he might find himself becoming a fan or player in the future. </p>
<p>Suddenly, there was yet another bang on the door. <i>Who is it now?</i></p>
<p>Three boys stood there and Harry immediately recognised the skinny, pale, blonde one from Madam Malkins. He was looking Harry up and down with extreme interest.</p>
<p>So its true? He asked with a raised eyebrow. Theyre all saying on the train that Harry Potter has come to Hogwarts. Its you, isnt it?</p>
<p>One and only. Harry narrowed his eyes at him. He still didnt like this boy.</p>
<p>This is Crabbe and Goyle. He gestured to his two bodyguards standing either side of him. And Im Malfoy. Draco Malfoy. He said snootily. </p>
<p>Ron let out a cough which may have been to hide a chuckle but Malfoy clearly caught him. </p>
<p>Think my name's funny, do you? No need to ask who you are. My father told me all the Weasleys have red hair, freckles, and more children than they can afford. He spat at him. Then he turned back to Harry. "You'll soon find out some wizarding families are much better than others, Potter. You don't want to go making friends with the wrong sort. I can help you there. He said, holding out his hand.</p>
<p>Harry looked down at his hand and up into his eyes, glaring. I think I can tell the wrong sort for myself, thanks. </p>
<p>I'd be careful if I were you, Potter. Malfoy said bitterly. Unless you're a bit politer, you'll go the same way as your parents. They didn't know what was good for them, either. You hang around with riffraff like the Weasleys and that Hagrid, and it'll rub off on you. He smirked.</p>
<p>Both Harry and Ron stood up, furious.</p>
<p>"Say that again, Malfoy! Ron yelled, his face as red as his hair. </p>
<p>"Oh, you're going to fight us, are you?" Malfoy sneered. </p>
<p>"Unless you get out now. Harry growled, feeling the beginning sparks of anger starting to rise up in him. He was still recovering from his full moon night, he doesnt need this. He didnt care that Crabbe and Goyle were a lot bigger than him or Ron, hed gladly tear them apart. </p>
<p>Calm down, deep breaths. </p>
<p>"But we don't feet like leaving, do we, boys? Malfoy said cockily.</p>
<p>Harry cracked his neck and took deep breaths. This prat had to go or hed put his head through a wall. Get out, Malfoy. He whispered dangerously. Now. He spat. </p>
<p>Something must have spooked him because Malfoys demeanour changed. He went from cocky aristocrat to scared kitty cat, backing up out of the compartment and down the hall, his bodyguards following him. </p>
<p>Ron slammed the door shut behind them and after a few seconds of silence, burst out laughing. </p>
<p>That was bloody brilliant!</p>
<p>What? Harry asked confused.  </p>
<p>You nearly made Malfoy wet himself! Ron grinned. You got this, like, dangerous look in your eye and I seriously thought you were gonna jump on him. Ron said, holding his chest dramatically. </p>
<p>So did I. Harry mumbled. He sat back down and rested his head against the window, taking calming breaths. Ron sat across from him and was still giggling, loving every second.</p>
<p>After five minutes, they decided to change into their school robes. Harrys were brand new and clean where Rons looked like hand-me-downs and they were a little on the short side, as you could see his shoes. </p>
<p>Soon, they heard a voice above:</p>
<p>
  <i>We will be reaching Hogwarts in five minutes time. Please leave your luggage on the train, it will be taken to the school separately.</i>
</p>
<p>They shrugged and left their stuff, heading down the hallways and towards the doors when the train eventually stopped. Harrys nerves were running wild, his stomach was churning. But Ron stayed by his side the entire time which made him happy.</p>
<p>They were on the platform outside, in the freezing cold, when suddenly he heard a familiar booming voice. </p>
<p>Firs years! Firs years, over here! All right there, Harry! Hagrid smiled at him, holding a lamp, big bushy beard flapping in the wind.</p>
<p>Hi, Hagrid. He smiled back, waving.</p>
<p>C'mon, follow me, any more firs' years? Mind yer step, now! Firs' years follow me!"</p>
<p>Nearly slipping on their backsides, they followed Hagrid down what seemed to be a steep, narrow path. It was so dark, nobody could see anything. The only source of light was from the lamp Hagrid was carrying. Nobody spoke much. Neville, the boy who kept losing his toad, whimpered every now and again.</p>
<p>"Yeh'll get yer firs' sight o' Hogwarts in a sec! Hagrid called over his shoulder. Jus' round this bend here. </p>
<p>Suddenly everyone gasped.</p>
<p>The path had finally opened onto the edge of the great black lake. Perched on top of a high mountain on the other side, the windows sparkling in the starry sky, towers pointing upwards, was a giant castle. Hogwarts. </p>
<p>"No more'n four to a boat!" Hagrid yelled out, pointing to all the little boats sitting in the water by the shore. Harry and Ron were followed into their boat by Neville and Hermione. </p>
<p>Ron rolled his eyes.</p>
<p>"Everyone in?" shouted Hagrid, who obviously had a boat to himself. Seeing everyone nod, he clapped his hands. Right then. Forward! </p>
<p>Suddenly, the boats starting moving towards the direction of the castle, everyone giggling and shrieking excitedly at what was going on.</p>
<p>They reached a cliff and Hagrid yelled at them to duck, so they wouldnt crack their heads open. They all swam inside what looked like a tunnel which was taking them underneath the castle, everyones voices bouncing off the walls.</p>
<p>Eventually they got off the boats, everyone trying to steady themselves. </p>
<p>Hagrid then let out a loud yelp. Oi, you there! Is this yer toad? He pointed to the boy named Neville and back at the little toad who was laying on the pebbles, croaking happily. </p>
<p>Trevor! Neville yelled happily, running forward and grabbing him gently. Whereve you been? </p>
<p>He didn get very far, now did e? Hagrid said with a smile. Nearly stepped on the wee bugger, mind ye. Hagrid said with a frown. </p>
<p>Well, thanks. Neville said, putting Trevor in his pocket for safety. Hagrid nodded and led them indoors, where they took a bunch of different turns until they walked up a giant staircase, stopping outside a room. </p>
<p>Everyone ere? He asked, looking around. He then turned towards the doors and knocked.</p>
