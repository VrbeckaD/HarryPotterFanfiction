    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p>
  <strong> <em>Flashbacks/dreams from original timeline</em> </strong>
</p><p>
  <em>Flashbacks/dreams from current timeline</em>
</p><p>
  <em>'thoughts'</em>
</p><p>m<strong><em>e</em></strong>r<strong><em>g</em></strong>i<strong><em>n</em></strong>g <em><strong>o</strong></em>f <em><strong>r</strong></em>e<strong><em>a</em></strong>l<strong><em>i</em></strong>t<strong><em>i</em></strong>e<strong><em>s  </em></strong>(Past/present)</p><p>Present.</p><hr/><p> </p><p> </p><p>Flashes.</p><p>That’s all her thoughts are – barely there images and sounds that slip away before she can make sense of them.</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>Running, running ...</b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>Her existence doesn’t make sense at the moment.</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>Screams, yells flashes of light...</b> </em>
</p><p> <em><b>A name:</b> <b>“HAZEL!”</b></em></p><p> </p><p>Is that her name? She’s not sure...there’s so many in the flashes...</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>FredHermioneRonTonksColinDeanRemusLavenderColinSirusLilyJames.....</b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>No. None of those are <u><em>her</em></u> name. They belong to other people....people dear to her...</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>Still bodies, lying in the centre of a hall....</b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>Something wet trails down her cheeks, a growing emptiness in her chest making her feel colder than she already is.</p><p> </p><p>“<em><b>I shall punish every last man, woman and child who has tried to conceal you from me...”</b></em></p><p> <em><b>This was her </b> </em> <b>home.</b></p><p> <em><b>Cold air...red eyes...</b></em></p><p> <em><b>Green light...</b></em></p><p> </p><p>Emerald green eyes snap open, and within seconds she is on her feet throwing herself at a magical barrier that her magic sensed....</p><p>She stumbles back, breathing heavy as her magic writhes and cries out in distress. A group of unfamiliar men dressed in dark cloaks stand outside the shinning barrier, gazing at her like she was a fascinating specimen.</p><p>She<b> </b><em><u>SCREAMS</u>.</em></p><p> </p><p align="center">I~I</p><p> </p><p>Time runs together.</p><p>Her holders – whoever they are – run test after test (some far more painful than others) and ask the same questions over and over and over...</p><p>“<em>What’s your name?”</em></p><p>“<em>Do you remember anything more today?”</em></p><p>“<em>How does this make you feel?”</em></p><p>From her, they always get more or less the same answer: “Why don’t you <u><em>fucking</em></u> tell me?”</p><p>It’s still written down on their stupid clipboards.</p><p>Every. Fucking. Time.</p><p>They never tell her why she’s there exactly, but she often hears them talking things about “Time” and “Dimensions” so it’s not hard for her to figure out the gist of it.</p><p>She’s not supposed to exist. It makes her <em><u>fascinating</u>. </em></p><p>It wouldn’t be such a problem, if being fascinating to<em> them</em> didn’t mean you no longer have many basic human rights.</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>A dark cupboard, a tiny worn mattress that sagged under her little weight...</b> </em>
</p><p>
  <strong>“FREAK!”</strong>
</p><p> </p><p>No. She knew she was human. The flashes told her that much. Human – with abilities that made electricity spark in the air, lashing out at her captives during particularly harsh tests.</p><p> </p><p>“<em><b>You’re a witch, Hazel.”</b></em></p><p> </p><p>A ball of golden light dances on her fingers.</p><p> </p><p align="center">I~I</p><p> </p><p>They are ever so curious, like children they poke and prod at every inch of her to watch her different reactions with gleeful excitement. </p><p>“<em><b>I killed...!”</b></em></p><p> </p><p>She stumbles backwards, spinning awkwardly as she forced her legs to <em>move away </em>from the two conversing before they noticed...</p><p> </p><p><em> <b>Fury she had never know...simmering...</b></em> <b>boiling</b> <b> </b> <em><b>her blood as she ran madly through the halls...</b> </em></p><p>“<em><b>Did you love him?”</b></em></p><p> </p><p>‘<em>Yes...’</em></p><p>She hears an inhuman moan and it takes her a moment to realize it was coming from <em>her</em> as hot tears trailed down her cheeks.</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>A boy with a bloody nose...shrieking in agony</b> </em>
</p><p>“<em><b>...Under arrest for the torture of Frank and Alice ....”</b></em></p><p>“<em><b>Crucio!”</b></em></p><p>“<em><b>You have to mean it...!!!”</b></em></p><p> </p><p>A shout...running footsteps.</p><p>She picks up speed.</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>Screams...</b> </em>
</p><p>
  <em> <b>A dark cellar</b> </em>
</p><p>
  <em> <b>Jagged writing in pale skin...<u>Mudblood</u></b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>The dirt is hard on her knees...rough between her fingernails...</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>A forest...</b> </em>
</p><p>“<em><b>My lord...”</b></em></p><p> </p><p>Her head is pounding...vision distorting as she scrambles to her feet. Before she had taken a step someone grasps her shoulders and pulls her back....</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>Feverish excitement in those cold dark eyes as she stood there...ready to...</b> </em>
</p><p>“<em><b>WHAT ARE YOU DOING HAZEL!”</b></em></p><p> </p><p>Queenie’s eyes are wide, filled with horror, shock and tears, but her grip is unrelenting...</p><p>“Hazel...”</p><p>There were too many flashes...too many feelings...</p><p>She sees a familiar blue coat flapping as the figure wearing it came closer...</p><p>A new emotion grips her then, and in a state of panic and delirium she grasps Queenie’s shoulder tightly...nails digging in with urgency:<em>“Don’t tell Newt.</em><em>"</em></p><p> </p><p align="center">I~I</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="left">She stares at the trembling boy with black smoke rising from his form not with fear or anger, but deep, terrible understanding...</p><p align="left"> </p><p>“<em><b>Parasitic growth...”</b></em></p><p>
  <em> <b>Hate...fear...anger...</b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>She steps nearer, forcing herself to ignore the sounds of Newt’s battle raging behind her...<em> ‘He’ll be fine...he’s all right... he better be...’</em></p><p>The thoughts pause when <em>t</em>he boy tenses. More smoke rising from his form...</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>A dead, slimy hand...</b> </em>
</p><p>
  <em> <b>A hideous gaping mouth...</b> </em>
</p><p>
  <em> <b>Those hands, colder than Death ever was...wrapping around her throat even though she was already choking on despair...</b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>She halts.</p><p>Dark, red rimmed eyes: “S-stay away!...I...”</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>A little girl in a cupboard cradling her sprained wrist, whispering wistfully “Happy Birthday...”</b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>Trembling lips...a cry:“I’m a freak!”</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> A young woman hidden behind a cell, drawing a mockery of a cake in the dirt despite her-keeper’s gifts making it hard... </em>
</p><p> </p><p>Anger, certainty: “No. No you aren’t Credence.”</p><p>But words aren’t enough. Words are lies without proof to one who has been lied to for their whole lives...</p><p> </p><p>“<em><b>A Patronus is a kind of positive force...hope, happiness, the desire to survive...”</b></em></p><p> </p><p>Emerald eyes flutter close:</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> Newt stares back, eyes bright and smile wide as she lay across his lap, drinking in the way the sunshine shone in his eyes... </em>
</p><p> </p><p>“<em><b>E</b></em>X<em><b>P</b></em>E<em><b>C</b></em>T<em><b>O </b></em>P<em><b>A</b></em>T<em><b>R</b></em>O<em><b>N</b></em>U<em><b>M!!:</b></em></p><p>A sliver doe and stag trot towards the black-haired boy, gently nudging him...</p><p> </p><p>“<em><b>The ones we love never truly leave us.”</b></em></p><p> </p><p>The boy jolts...shrinking in for only a second before the fear begins to fade...</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>Letters, so, so many letters...and that girl in overgrown clothes is jumping – beaming because they are proof that someone else out there knows her...</b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>A pale hand reached out...a puff of breath escapes Credence’s mouth: “What is this? I...feel... <em>happy</em><em>."</em></p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>The room was filled with silvery forms and the laughter of her friends and for a moment she could imagine there was no war...that they could be children again...</b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> Sky-sea eyes filled with wonder as the phoenix flew over to perch on his arm... </em>
</p><p> </p><p>“What magic is supposed to be.”</p><p> </p><p align="left"> </p><p align="center">I~I</p><p> </p><p> <em><b>Running...</b></em>Running...</p><p> </p><p>Always running towards the screams...</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>Towards the battle.</b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>Towards Death.</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>Red hair...wonderful laughter: “You’re joking...!”</b> </em>
</p><p>
  <em> <b>Explosion...the silence. Smile, but no laughter...</b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>A thrashing body in a blue coat...</p><p>A man on the platform, relentlessly hitting him with lightning...</p><p> </p><p>
  <em> <b>White masks...</b> </em>
</p><p>
  <em> <b>A green skull in the sky...</b> </em>
</p><p> </p><p>Red in her vision, wand aimed towards the man’s feet</p><p>“REDUCTO!”</p><p>Rubble...crashing...and the man is flying as she runs, runs, run toward the blue coat...</p><p> </p><p>“<em><b>I’M DONE! I WANT IT TO END!”</b></em></p><p> </p><p>Dazed eyes, slurring speech... “Hazel...what...?”</p><p>Forced calm, words between healing spells: “Credence is safe.”</p><p>“Credence...? No. You...<em>you</em> need to get out of here!” Realization...desperation...a hand closing around her wrist...</p><p> </p><p>“<em><b>You have permitted your friends to die for you rather than face me yourself...”</b></em></p><p> </p><p>Tears in her eyes, fury bubbling in her voice: <em>“No.”</em></p><p>Sitting up, hardening gaze – sincere concern:<em>“Hazel...”</em></p><p>Anger. Desperation. <em><u>Love</u>...</em></p><p>
  <em> “I’ve seen enough people die!” </em>
</p><p>Shock. Silence...</p><p>Then, explosions....</p><p> </p><p align="center"> I~I</p><p align="center"> </p><p align="left"><em> <b>S</b></em>p<em><b>e</b></em><b></b>l<strong><em>l</em></strong>s..</p><p align="left"> <em><strong>R</strong></em>e<strong><em>d</em></strong>, p<em><b>u</b></em>r<em><b>p</b></em>l<em><b>e, </b></em>w<strong><em>h</em></strong>i<strong><em>t</em></strong>e...<em><b> g</b></em>r<em><b>e</b></em>e<em><b>n</b></em></p><p align="left"><em><b>C</b></em>ra<em><b>c</b></em>k<em><b>e</b></em>d <em><b>g</b></em>r<em><b>o</b></em>u<em><b>n</b></em>d -<em><b>r</b></em>u<em><b>b</b></em>b<em><b>l</b></em>e</p><p align="left"><em><b>W</b></em>h<em><b>i</b></em>t<em><b>e</b></em> k<em><b>n</b></em>u<em><b>c</b></em>k<em><b>l</b></em>e<em><b>s</b></em> – w<em><b>a</b></em>r<em><b>m</b></em> w<em><b>a</b></em>n<em><b>d</b></em>...</p><p align="left">A spell...the wand is gone...</p><p align="left">For the first time, Graves smirks, gloating: “What’s a witch without a wand?”</p><p align="left">‘<em>I’ll show you.’</em></p><p align="left"> <em><b>F</b></em>l<em><b>y</b></em>i<em><b>n</b></em>g, <em><b>f</b></em>l<em><b>y</b></em>i<em><b>n</b></em>g towards the smug man...fists clenches...lips snarling...</p><p align="left"> She punches the smirk in. Then does it again.</p><p align="left"> Again.</p><p align="left"> Again.</p><p align="left"> Again.</p><p align="left"> He tries to throw her off...going so far as to change in a misty form to fly around the station...</p><p align="left"> She holds on, scratching...<em>clawing </em>at him...</p><p align="left"> <em><b>B</b></em>l<em><b>o</b></em>o<em><b>d </b></em>b<em><b>e</b></em>n<em><b>e</b></em>a<em><b>t</b></em>h <em><b>h</b></em>e<em><b>r </b></em>n<em><b>a</b></em>i<em><b>l</b></em>s...</p><p align="left"> He yells: “ENOUGH!”</p><p align="left"> A blast throws her off..she tumbles into rubble...</p><p align="left"> <em><b>H</b></em>e<b>a</b>v<em><b>y </b></em>b<em><b>r</b></em>e<em><b>a</b></em>t<em><b>h</b></em>e<em><b>s...</b></em></p><p align="left"> <em><b>T</b></em>h<em><b>e t</b></em>a<em><b>s</b></em>t<em><b>e </b></em>o<em><b>f </b></em>b<em><b>l</b></em>o<em><b>o</b></em>d..</p><p align="left"> A force yanks her back into the air, squeezing on her windpipe...</p><p align="left">Shouts. Panic...and she sees Newt pounding on the shield she had placed around the area...eyes wild, hair a mess...</p><p align="left">Cold fingers grip her chin, forcing emerald orbs to meet cold black filled with interest: “Why are you fighting me, girl? We could achieve so much together...”</p><p align="left"> </p><p align="left">“<em><b>...There is only power and those to weak to seek it...”</b></em></p><p align="left">“<em><b>LIAR!”</b></em></p><p align="left"> </p><p align="left">Despite his grip, emerald eyes manage to meet sky-sea brimming with tears and fury... “There are better things worth fighting for than power.”</p><p align="left">Loud, bitter laughter. “Let me guess, Friendship?”</p><p align="left"> </p><p align="left">
  <em> <b>A boy with a bloody nose running down stone steps.“She’s dot alone! She’s still god be!...”</b> </em>
</p><p align="left">
  <em> Jacob unsuccessfully trying keep in his laughter as they watched Newt perform the mating dance... </em>
</p><p align="left"> </p><p align="left">“Forgiveness?”</p><p align="left"> </p><p align="left">

</p><p align="left">

</p><p align="left"> </p><p align="left">

</p><p align="left"> </p><p align="left">
