        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p>Harry stared out at the Forbidden Forest, watching as the stars and the moon came out over the castle. The astronomy tower was the highest tower at Hogwarts and he had it to himself to the night, hopefully. His heart still ached and he had barely touched dinner, making Hermione and Ron worry. </p><p>Albus Dumbledore was dead and Harry didn’t know what to make of it.</p><p>His heart fell into his stomach as he leaned against the wall next to him, memories flashing through his mind. Draco confronting Dumbledore… Snape killing him. The Dark Mark being cast as Snape and the other Death Eaters fled the castle.</p><p>RAB. The locket that he and Dumbledore had found in Tom’s secret cave.</p><p>The sound of another strangled gasp startled him so badly he nearly fell off the edge as he turned to look beside him. A man shimmered into view next to him, his clothes strange enough that Harry raised an eyebrow, mind momentarily drawn away. </p><p>The man wore white robes and a hood, with a beaked cowl. There was a sword at his waist and what Harry figured to be a few knives at his belt too.
