    <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Chapter Text</h3>
    <p>It’s been over a year since I posted to this story and I am sorry. I lost a bit of my inspiration and am actually still having trouble ending year three. Truthfully I have grand plans for all the years except year three and it is taking me a long time and multiple discarded drafts to settle on a plot that I like.</p><p>In that time, I went back and cleaned up Books 1 and 2. You don’t need to re-read them as I only did minor additions and a lot of grammar corrections. The plot wasn’t changed. I just expanded the Minerva McGonagall and the Amazons storyline a little.</p><p>The Changes:</p><p>- I have changed all measurements to standard because JK Rowling mentioned in an interview that the   </p><p>  Wizarding world would not had made the transition to metric. And because I’m American</p><p>- Harry’s full title is His Grace Lord Harry James Potter, Duke of Crestvale, Earl of Winterfall, Baron of</p><p>   Glintshire and Knight Magica of the Order of the Eagle.</p><p>- The four elite houses, collectivity make up the Prima Potentia Magicae</p><p>- The New Amazons and Minerva McGonagall got fleshed out more.</p><p>They have a floating island (Themyscira) that they built on McGonagall’s property. The land that was dug out for the island is filled with water and called “Lake Diana”.</p><p>Themyscira is open to the public and considered a New Wonder of the Magical World.</p><p>Amazons developed a potion for super strength (Hecate’s Blessing) and a pool to refine beauty (Aphrodite’s Pond)</p><p>Pomona Sprout and Poppy Pomphrey join McGonagall’s family as concubines</p><p>-  Remus has two additional members to his harem</p><p>                Blanche Delacour is a Veela and Fleur’s aunt.</p><p>                Kivita is a Vampire and used to be Rita Skeeter</p><p>- Sirius also has two additional members to his primary Harem</p><p>                Aubrey (Black Atlantean)</p><p>                Sharon (Japanese)</p><p>- I have started to take notes on how I describe people.</p><p>Some descriptions might not match up but they will be the consistent from this point on.</p><p>- Harry never took Fawkes’ blood at the end of Book 2.</p><p> </p><p>Below are the families as they existed at the End of Book 2, if you need a reminder.</p><p align="center">
