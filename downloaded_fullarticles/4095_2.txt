          <div class="userstuff"><h1 align="center">
  <strong>Hear!</strong>
</h1><p align="center">O scholar-mages sound, to school you spring</p><p align="center">This Hogwarts hall, with hearth-fire and heart</p><p align="center">To learn of lore, in lessons long</p><p align="center">Of magic mysteries, and magic means</p><p align="center">In walls of wizardry, of witchcraft wise</p><p align="center">Four founders age, and fear their frailty</p><p align="center">To solve this sorting, scraped and sought</p><p align="center">And found their fix, to fearsome fuss</p><p align="center">A headcap handy, heavy-laden of hoarded lore</p><p align="center">With mage-craft made, magnificent of mind</p><p align="center">Their students sort, sage-quest of speaking hat</p><p align="center">Each mage-house made, and mage-scholars to make</p><p align="center">School-houses separated, each sort of special-kind</p><p align="center">Each founder's to find, these fact-seeking first-years</p><p align="center">Bold Godric Gryffindor, of grave-wands great</p><p align="center">A flaming fire-brand, and fearsome field-kings</p><p align="center">He brings each the bravest, the bold and blade-mind</p><p align="center">Wise Rowena Ravenclaw, of radiant written-words</p><p align="center">Abundant of air-lore, and air-ruling agile-eyes </p><p align="center">The wit-laden of word-craft, the wisest welcomed</p><p align="center">Keen Salazar Slytherin, of serpent-tongued speech</p><p align="center">With sea-lore secrets, and silent serpent-words</p><p align="center">The goal-seekers guileful, of generations great-magical</p><p align="center">Kind Helga Hufflepuff, heart-opened hope</p><p align="center">To sage-bearing solid-earth, with stripe-faced stalwart</p><p align="center">All teach-seekers to teach-well, and trust to toil-hardy</p><p align="center">Four school-houses steadfast, now select their stake</p><p align="center">This wise-hat wear, and wend-home to your welcome</p></div>
