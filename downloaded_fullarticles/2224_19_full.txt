          <div class="userstuff"><p>
  <span>The first time I tell him he’s mine, he’s reading some long winded report from one of his minions. The sun is shining through a half-open window, back-lighting him so he looks, for the moment, like a God. </span>
</p><p>He likes sitting in front of that large window for that reason, I think. It makes him look even more impressive than he already is.</p><p>He looks up at me, surprised and amused. “Oh?” is all he says.</p><p>I stalk across the room, my legs tired from sitting cross-legged all morning, my left foot has the beginnings of pins and needles. None of that matters though. I feel like a panther ready to pounce on its prey.</p><p>
  <span>“I’m your Horcrux,” I remind him, not that he needs it. The elegant silver collar around my neck is proof of that. “I’m your Horcrux, which makes you </span>
  <em>
    <span>mine.</span>
  </em>
  <span>”</span>
