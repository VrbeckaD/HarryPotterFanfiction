      <p>You can’t help your grin, having known it was only a matter of time before he would come calling on you for help. You turn to see the wizard, his platinum hair falling over his shoulders, his head held high as if demanding the world be aware of his elite lineage. You’re not surprised he doesn’t present himself humbly even to you, it wasn’t Lucius Malfoy’s way.</p>
      <p>You ask the question you already know the answer to, your grin widening a fraction, “What brings you to me, Lucius? Don’t you know, all magic comes with a price!”</p>
