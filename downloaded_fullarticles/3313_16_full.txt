That didn’t phase Drax though, who continued on with his verbal assaults.</p><p> </p><p>“Black, control your tongue! While Slughorn might let you get away with such outbursts, I will not! Heat was rushing to his face and he bowed his head, trying to hide from his classmates. A lot of them were glancing at him curiously, trying to figure out which one of them he was talking about. Remus decided to ignore them, at least until he came to Severus, then he allowed his gaze to slowed. But only slightly. No one else but the two of them seemed to notice. Severus, unlike the rest of them, looked livid. It wasn’t until he broke a cauldron on the ground that he had the class’ undivided attention and began teaching again.</p><p> </p><p><span class="Apple-converted-space">         </span>It was around lunch time that Remus was starting to feel the effects of potions. Bartemius even went as far to grab Remus by his waist, saying that he was honored to be Remus’ boyfriend. Which prompted a loud outbreak of laughs and shouts, resulting in an irritated Flinch to come marching down the halls. Bartemius quickly let go, leaving Remus alone in the halls again.</p><p> </p><p>“Who would’ve thought that he’d be so hot with another boy,” Remus heard a soft voice mumble from somewhere down the hall.</p><p> </p><p>“I know right, he should’ve gone gay a while back. I’m not interested in you,” Remus hissed, noticing how eyes were starting to stare in their direction.</p><p> </p><p>“Maybe not, but you looked rather lonely Remy. Remus didn’t have much longer to ponder on it seeing as Bartemius closed the gap.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p> </p><p><span class="Apple-converted-space">         </span>Whimpering at the aggressive nips from Bartemius, Remus realized that he was beginning to bring in a crowd. Bartemius seemed determined to give them a show and Remus felt himself being pushed down on the bench. A heavy weight pressing on his stomach caused Remus to gasp loudly and he noticed that Bartemius took it upon himself to straddle Remus. In a weak attempt to push him off, Remus pulled his legs up and bucked his arms against his shoulders. But Remus was scrawny and his efforts were proven to be worthless. Eventually though, Bartemius pulled up. There was hoots and whistles coming from the crowd and Remus jumped up the second he was able to, successfully knocking Bartemius to the ground.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p> </p><p>“Stay away from me Bartemius!” Remus shouted at the younger yet embarrassingly bigger boy.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span></p><p> </p><p>“Yeah right Lupin, it looked like you were enjoying the attention that Barty was giving you,” a ravenclaw called out from the side, causing snickers to erupt from the crowd.</p><p> </p><p><span class="Apple-converted-space">         </span>Remus glared at them, ready to shout back but another voice beat him to it, “If he says he didn’t want it then obviously he didn’t want it.”</p><p> </p><p><span class="Apple-converted-space">         </span>All malicious intent left Remus’ mind as he locked eyes with Severus.
