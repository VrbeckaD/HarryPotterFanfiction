        <h3 class="landmark heading" id="work">Work Text:</h3>
          <div class="userstuff"><p><strong>Once upon a time,</strong> there was a small village plagued by a curse. The only one who could lift the curse was a terrible beast who lived in the forest. But lifting such a curse every year came with a steep price as well, and every six months, the villagers would send one of their own out to meet the monster, never to be heard from again.</p><p>In this village lived a young man named Harry, who had two friends whom he loved dearly. They sat near the lake, and Harry watched silently as Ron picked at the grass of the bank.</p><p>"They're picking another sacrifice again," Hermione said quietly.
