          <div class="userstuff"><p><i>Led to Dumbledore’s death.</i> The thought echoed, though Harry had phrased it differently, though he was still talking. </p><p>Regulus ought to listen, as much for personal affection as expediency, but he could scarcely think past the rush in his ears. Dumbledore might merit scorn for his opinions, but never for his skills. He wanted to laugh; he wanted to call for Kreacher, to tell his mother that all would be well: he would expunge all his brother’s wrongdoings. </p><p>Instead, he leaned in, on the surge of affection for this man who had come to help him, and kissed him hard.</p></div>
