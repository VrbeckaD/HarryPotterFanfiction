    <p>The feeling of something falling on his cheek almost sent Merlin into a tailspin, too many times had the birds of the forest ruthlessly attacked him and he refused have this, their first real romantic outing marred by bloody pigeons. Arthur’s bold laugh echoed through the empty clearing as he watched Merlin swipe desperately at his cheek only to realise that his fingers came away cold and clean. “The first snow of the year.” Arthur whispered softly, and quick as the changing wind he ducked down to place a kiss where the snowflake had landed. Merlin’s cheeks weren’t cold anymore.</p>
