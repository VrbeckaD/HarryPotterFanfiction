    <p><b>SHE KNEW THAT THIS WASN'T RIGHT</b>, but god did it feel so <em>good</em>.</p><p>Tricking that Cullen boy was a walk in the park, and it gave her this new thing called <em>power</em>.</p><p>Power to what? </p><p>Immortality was a bitch, but Bella has always been alone.</p><p> </p><p align="center"><b>(</b> <em>so who cared? </em><b>)</b></p><p> </p><p>Draining blood from magical creatures gave her new abilities, and magic was really useful whenever she found herself in a pickle!</p><p>No, what that boy gave her was <em>time</em>.</p><p>She found out she could time travel, escape when needed, learn from people that herself from the future used to know only from the internet. <em>So part of her owed the Cullen kid</em>.</p><p>Not that it really mattered, not since the boy walked out her life and left her squib father to pick up the pieces.</p><p>Why did she try to go to muggle school after completing Hogwarts anyway? At first, when it hurted, she thought she ruined her life. Then, as if a flip was switched, she thought:</p><p> </p><p align="center"><b>"</b> <em> Why not get my revenge? </em> <b>"</b></p><p> </p><p>She did it out of spite, but the Volturi were <em>very understanding</em>.
